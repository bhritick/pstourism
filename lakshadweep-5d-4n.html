Lakshadweep Trip - 4 Nights, 5 Days

Join PST Holidayz by Pather Sandhane Tourism (ISO 9001:2015 Certified) for an unforgettable Lakshadweep experience.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Agatti Island

Upon arrival at Agatti Airport via Air India/IndiGo, youâ€™ll be greeted by our representative and escorted to your hotel. Enjoy a refreshing welcome drink followed by lunch. In the afternoon, embark on a snorkeling adventure with our water sports team.

Day 2: Agatti to Thinnakara Islands (Day Trip)

Take a private boat ride to the uninhabited Thinnakara Islands for a day of exploring pristine beaches, swimming with turtles, and snorkeling. The trip includes a visit to a shipwreck site and sandbanks near Bangaram Island (weather permitting).

Day 3: Water Sports Activities

Enjoy a day of water sports at Agatti, including a glass-bottom boat ride, banana boat ride, and kayaking. Visit Kalpitti Island for turtle-watching and a scenic walk.

Day 4: South Beach Water Sports

Head to South Beach for SCUBA diving, snorkeling, and kayaking, with additional water activities available for an extra fee. The beach offers beautiful underwater scenery and a peaceful escape.

Day 5: Departure

After breakfast, complete the departure formalities and head to Agatti Airport for your return flight to Kochi.
