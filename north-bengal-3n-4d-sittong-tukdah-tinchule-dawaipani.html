3 Nights 4 Days North Bengal Package

Explore Sittong, Tukdah, Darjeeling with Pather Sandhane Tourism (ISO 9001:2015 certified).

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Transfer to Sittong

Pick-up from your location and transfer to Sittong. Visit Namthing Pokhri, Mahaldhiram Tea Garden, and Ahaldhara Viewpoint. Overnight stay at Sittong.

Day 2: Transfer to Tukdah / Tinchule / Dawaipani

Proceed to the offbeat destinations of Tukdah/Tinchule/Dawaipani. Sightseeing includes Yogighat Bridge and Rabindra Bhawan. Overnight stay at Tukdah/Tinchule/Dawaipani.

Day 3: Transfer to Darjeeling

Transfer to Darjeeling with visits to Lamahata Eco Park, Tukdah Tinchule Viewpoint, and Batasia Loop. Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 4: NJP Drop

On your final day, visit Mirik Lake, Pashupati Market, Simana Viewpoint, and Bokar Monastery. Drop at NJP.

Package Inclusions & Exclusions

Inclusions:

  • All quality meals (from Day 1 lunch to check-out day breakfast)
  • Standard/Deluxe/Premium lodging as per preference
  • Private 4-seater cab for all transfers and sightseeing
  • Driver allowance and other transportation-related costs

Exclusions:

  • Entry fees, tickets, and parking charges
  • Food during the journey
  • Any expenses not mentioned in the package
  • Charges for changes in the planned itinerary or route diversions
