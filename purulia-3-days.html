Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Exploration

  • Pick-up: Our team will meet you at Borabhum Station.
  • Sightseeing: Begin your trip with visits to the beautiful Pardi Dam, the birdwatcher's paradise Pakhi Pahar, and the scenic Matha Pahar.
  • Accommodation: Check into your comfortable homestay at Ayodhya, Purulia, offering a serene ambiance for a relaxing evening.

Day 2: Discover the Hidden Gems of Purulia

  • Morning Excursion: Visit the spiritual sites of Loharia Mandir and explore Kestobazar Dam, including its upper and lower sections. Enjoy the tranquility of Sita Kunda.
  • Afternoon Exploration: Dive deeper into Purulia's natural beauty with visits to Durga Mandir, Ram Mandir, the lush Mayur Pahar, and the scenic dams and waterfallsâ€”Murguma Dam, Marble Lake, Bamni Falls, Thurga Falls, and Thurga Dam.
  • Return: After a fulfilling day of exploration, return to your homestay at the foothills of Ajodhya.

Day 3: Joychandi Pahar and Departure

  • Excursion: Start your day with a visit to the iconic Joychandi Pahar, followed by Baranti Lake, the massive Panchet Dam, and the historical site of Garhpanchakot.
  • Drop-off: Our team will ensure your comfortable transfer to Purulia Station for your journey back home.

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation Options: Choose between Standard, Deluxe, or Premium rooms in hotels or tents at all locations, based on your preferences.
  • Meal Plan: Enjoy a complete AP Meal Plan that covers Breakfast, Lunch, Evening Snacks, and Dinner at your homestay.
  • Transfers & Sightseeing: Private vehicle for all sightseeing, pick-up, and drop-off as per the itinerary.
  • Experienced Drivers and Guides: Expert drivers ensure a safe journey, with an optional guide available to enrich your travel experience.
  • Bonfire: Enjoy a cozy bonfire experience wherever possible (spot payment is advised).

Exclusions

  • Entry fees, parking, tickets for attractions.
  • Food during travel or any additional items not mentioned in the itinerary.
