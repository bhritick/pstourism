Offbeat North Bengal with Darjeeling - 4 Nights, 5 Days Tour

Explore the beauty of Sittong, Darjeeling & Mirik with Pather Sandhane Tourism

Day 1 - Arrival at NJP / Bagdogra and Transfer to Sittong

Arrival and transfer to Sittong, famous for its orange orchards. En route, visit:

  • Namthing Pokhri
  • Sittong Orange Orchards
  • Mahaldhiram Tea Garden
  • Waterfalls
  • Yogighat Bridge
  • Mongpu Rabindra Museum

Overnight stay in Sittong.

Day 2 - Sittong to Darjeeling via Lamahatta Eco Park

On the way to Darjeeling, visit:

  • Lamahatta Eco Park
  • Takdah
  • Tinchule View Point
  • Ahaldhara View Point

Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 3 - Darjeeling Sightseeing

  • Tiger Hill for Sunrise
  • Peace Pagoda
  • Japanese Temple
  • Tenzing Rock
  • Rangeet Valley Tea Garden
  • Darjeeling Zoo
  • Himalayan Mountaineering Institute
  • Batasia Loop
  • Ghoom Monastery

Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 4 - Darjeeling to Mirik

On the way to Mirik, visit:

  • Gopaldhara Tea Estate
  • Mirik Lake
  • Pashupati Market (Nepal border)
  • Bokar Monastery
  • Simana View Point

Overnight stay in Mirik.

Day 5 - Departure to NJP / Bagdogra

After breakfast, transfer to NJP Railway Station or Bagdogra Airport for your return journey.
