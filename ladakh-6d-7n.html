Itinerary - "Unforgettable Ladakh"

  • Day 01: Arrival at Leh and acclimatization. Stay indoors, stay hydrated, and prepare for the adventure ahead. Overnight at Shey/Leh.
  • Day 02: Leh Local Sightseeing: Visit Gurudwara Pathar Sahib, Spituk Monastery, Leh Palace, Shey Palace, Shanti Stupa, and explore Leh Market. Overnight at Shey/Leh.
  • Day 03: Travel to Hundar via Khardungla Pass. Overnight at Homestay/Tent/Hotel in Hunder/Deskit.
  • Day 04: Day trip to Turtuk, the last village with tourist permits in Nubra Valley. Overnight at Hunder/Deskit.
  • Day 05: Travel from Hunder/Deskit to Pangong Tso Lake. Visit the famous 3 Idiots Point and the beautiful Pangong Lake. Overnight at Pangong Tent/Camp.
  • Day 06: Pangong Tso to Shey/Leh via Chang-la Pass. Visit Changla Baba Temple. Overnight at Shey/Leh.
  • Day 07: Departure from Shey/Leh. After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your flight home.
