Tour Details

2 Nights Darjeeling, 3 Nights Gangtok, 2 Nights North Sikkim, with 1 Day East Sikkim sightseeing - Tosamo Lake, Baba Mandir, Nathula Pass.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1 - Arrival & Transfer to Rangbhang

Pickup from NJP Station / Airport and transfer to offbeat Mirik, Rangbhang. Check in at Hotel/Homestay and enjoy your evening on the banks of Rangbhang River.

Day 2 - Mirik Sightseeing & Transfer to Darjeeling

Explore Mirik, visiting the following locations:

  • Gopaldhara Tea Estate
  • Mirik Lake
  • Pashupati Market
  • Bokar Monastery
  • Simana View Point

Evening at Darjeeling Mall Market. Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 3 - Darjeeling Sightseeing

Early morning visit to Tiger Hill for a stunning sunrise view. Post-breakfast sightseeing includes:

  • Batasia Loop
  • Ghoom Monastery
  • Peace Pagoda
  • Japanese Temple
  • Tenzing Rock
  • Rangeet Valley Tea Garden
  • Zoo, Museum, HMI

Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 4 - Free Day in Darjeeling

Enjoy a free day for a Darjeeling Joy Ride or explore the local areas. Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 5 - Transfer to Gangtok

Pickup from Darjeeling and proceed to Gangtok. Explore the High Court Museum located beside the hotel. In the evening, visit MG Marg and Lal Market. Overnight stay in Gangtok.

Day 6 - North Sikkim Exploration

Start the journey to North Sikkim with a transfer to Lachen, including sightseeing on the way. Overnight stay in Lachen.

Day 7 - Lachen to Lachung

After sightseeing in Lachen, transfer to Lachung, visiting Gurudongmar Lake along the way. Overnight stay in Lachung.

Day 8 - Yumthang Valley & Return to Gangtok

Explore the beautiful Yumthang Valley and Zero Point before transferring back to Gangtok. Overnight stay in Gangtok.

Day 9 - Rest Day or East Sikkim Exploration

This day can be reserved for rest, or you may opt to explore East Sikkim with visits to Changu Lake, Baba Mandir, and Nathula Pass. Overnight stay in Gangtok or prepare for departure the next day.

Day 10 - Departure

After breakfast, you will be dropped at NJP Station or the Airport for your onward journey.
