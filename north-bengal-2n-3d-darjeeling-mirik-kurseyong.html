2 Nights, 3 Days North Bengal Trip

Organized by PST Holidayz in collaboration with Pather Sandhane Tourism

An ISO 9001:2015 Certified Travel Organization

Book Now

Pick-up & Drop Locations: NJP (New Jalpaiguri Railway Station) / IXB (Bagdogra Airport) / Siliguri (SLG)

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Scenic Sightseeing

Pick-Up: Warm welcome at NJP, Siliguri, or IXB, followed by transfer to Darjeeling.

En-Route Sightseeing: Mirik Lake, Pasupati Market, Simana View Point, Bokar Monastery, Gopal Dhara Tea Garden.

Overnight Stay: Accommodation in Darjeeling.

Day 2: Full Day of Darjeeling Sightseeing

Explore major attractions: Tiger Hill, Batasia Loop, Ghoom Monastery, Peace Pagoda, Japanese Temple, Darjeeling Zoo, Ropeway, Rangeet Valley Tea Garden, Tenzing Rock.

Overnight Stay: Return to your accommodation in Darjeeling.

Day 3: Departure with a Touch of Kurseong

Check-Out: After breakfast, check out from your hotel.

En-Route Sightseeing: Kurseong scenic spots and tea gardens.

Drop-Off: Drop at NJP, IXB, or Siliguri.

Inclusions

  • All meals from lunch on Day 1 to breakfast on Day 3.
  • Comfortable accommodation in quality hotels.
  • All transfers and sightseeing in a personal cab.
  • Driver allowance and experienced hill driving.

Exclusions

  • Entry fees, tickets, or parking charges.
  • Additional food costs during the journey.
  • Extra expenses due to sudden changes in plans or routes.
  • Anything not explicitly mentioned in the package.
