2 Nights, 3 Days North Bengal Trip

Organized by PST Holidayz in collaboration with Pather Sandhane Tourism

An ISO 9001:2015 certified travel organization

Website: www.pstourism.in

Pick-up & Drop Locations: NJP (New Jalpaiguri Railway Station) / IXB (Bagdogra Airport) / Siliguri (SLG)

Explore the Beauty of North Bengal

Join us for an unforgettable 2-night, 3-day adventure in the breathtaking landscapes of North Bengal. This carefully curated trip offers a blend of scenic beauty, cultural experiences, and serene relaxation. Our experienced team at PST Holidayz is committed to providing you with a memorable journey.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Scenic Sightseeing

  • Pick-Up: Your journey begins with a warm welcome as we pick you up from NJP, Siliguri, or IXB.
  • Transfer to Darjeeling: Enjoy a picturesque drive to Darjeeling, known for its stunning views and pleasant climate.
  • En-Route Sightseeing:
    • Mirik Lake: A tranquil lake surrounded by lush hills, perfect for a leisurely stroll or a boat ride.
    • Pasupati Market: A vibrant market located at the Indo-Nepal border, offering a variety of goods and local crafts.
    • Simana View Point: Marvel at panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.
    • Bokar Monastery: Visit this peaceful monastery known for its serene environment and spiritual ambiance.
    • Gopal Dhara Tea Garden: Take a guided tour through the sprawling tea gardens, learning about tea cultivation and processing.
  • Overnight Stay: Relax at your comfortable accommodation in Darjeeling, soaking in the cool mountain air.

Day 2: Full Day of Darjeeling Sightseeing

  • Explore Darjeeling: After breakfast, prepare for a full day of sightseeing:
    • Tiger Hill: Witness the spectacular sunrise over the Kanchenjunga, the third highest peak in the world.
    • Batasia Loop: Enjoy a unique experience as you see the toy train navigate this spiral track amidst beautiful gardens.
    • Ghoom Monastery: Visit one of the oldest Tibetan monasteries in the region, known for its stunning Buddha statue.
    • Peace Pagoda: A symbol of peace, this structure offers breathtaking views and a calm atmosphere.
    • Japanese Temple: Experience the serene surroundings and beautiful architecture of this iconic temple.
    • Darjeeling Zoo and Ropeway: Discover local wildlife and enjoy a scenic ride offering stunning views of the hills and valleys.
    • Rangeet Valley Tea Garden: Explore more tea gardens and savor the fresh mountain air.
    • Tenzing Rock: For adventure enthusiasts, try your hand at rock climbing on this renowned training site.
  • Overnight Stay: Return to your accommodation for a restful night after a day filled with exploration.

Day 3: Departure with a Touch of Kurseong

  • Check-Out: After a delicious breakfast, check out from your hotel.
  • En-Route Sightseeing: On your way back, we’ll explore some scenic spots in Kurseong, known for its picturesque views and tea gardens.
  • Drop-Off: Finally, we will drop you at NJP, IXB, or Siliguri, concluding your trip with wonderful memories.

Inclusions

  • Meals: Enjoy all quality meals, starting from lunch on Day 1 to breakfast on the day of departure.
  • Accommodation: Comfortable lodging in quality hotels to ensure a pleasant stay.
  • Transportation: All sightseeing transfers, pick-up, and drop-off in a personal cab for your convenience.
  • Driver Allowance: Experienced drivers familiar with hill driving to ensure a safe journey.

Exclusions

  • Any entry fees, tickets, or parking charges.
  • Additional food costs during the journey.
  • Extra expenses arising from sudden changes in plans or routes.
  • Anything not explicitly mentioned in the package.
