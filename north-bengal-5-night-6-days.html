5 Days 4 Nights Kalimpong & Darjeeling Trip

Experience the beauty of Kalimpong and Darjeeling with Pather Sandhane Tourism (ISO 9001:2015 certified).

Book Now

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Transfer to Kalimpong

Upon arrival at NJP Station or Bagdogra Airport, you'll be transferred to a serene homestay in an offbeat location in Kalimpong. Overnight stay with AP meal plan (All meals included).

Day 2: Kalimpong Sightseeing

Explore Kalimpong’s attractions including Delo Park, Morgan House, Durpin Monastery, Cactus Garden, and Hanuman Top. Return to your homestay in the evening. Overnight stay with AP meal plan.

Day 3: Transfer to Darjeeling (En Route Sightseeing)

After breakfast, head towards Darjeeling. Visit Gumbadhara Viewpoint, Lover's Meet Point, Lamahata Eco Park, and Tinchule Viewpoint along the way. Overnight stay in Darjeeling with AP meal plan.

Day 4: Leisure Day in Darjeeling

Spend the day at your leisure, exploring Darjeeling at your own pace. You can enjoy a walk along Mall Road or sip tea at a local café. Overnight stay with AP meal plan.

Day 5: Darjeeling Sightseeing

Explore local attractions including Batasia Loop, Ghoom Monastery, Peace Pagoda, and Darjeeling Zoo. Visit the Rangeet Valley Tea Garden for a refreshing experience. Overnight stay with AP meal plan.

Day 6: Departure via Mirik

After breakfast, check out and head towards NJP Station or Bagdogra Airport. En route, visit the scenic town of Mirik and enjoy its serene lake and surrounding greenery. Drop-off at the station/airport.

Package Inclusions & Exclusions

Inclusions:

  • Accommodation with AP meal plan (All meals included)
  • Private vehicle for transfers and sightseeing
  • Guided sightseeing tours

Exclusions:

  • Entry fees and parking charges
  • Personal expenses
  • Optional activities not included in the itinerary
