About Sikkim

Sikkim, nestled in the Himalayas, offers a breathtaking landscape with snow-capped peaks, lush forests, and serene lakes, making it a haven for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike. From trekking in the mighty Kanchenjunga to exploring ancient monasteries like Rumtek, the state offers diverse experiences. Its rich cultural heritage, vibrant festivals, and warm hospitality make it a must-visit destination for tourists seeking an authentic Himalayan experience.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in West Sikkim

Transfer from NJP to Pelling. Overnight stay in Pelling.

Day 2: West Sikkim Exploration

Sightseeing of Pelling, including:

  • Rimbi River
  • Rimbi Orange Garden
  • Khecheopalri Lake
  • Pemayangtse Monastery
  • India's Highest Glass Sky Walk

Overnight stay in Pelling.

Day 3: Transfer to Ravangla

Transfer from Pelling to Ravangla via Namchi Chardham. Overnight stay in Ravangla.

Day 4: Transfer to Gangtok

Transfer to Gangtok via Temi Tea Garden and Buddha Park. Overnight stay in Gangtok.

Day 5: North Sikkim Exploration

Transfer to Lachung with sightseeing. Overnight stay in Lachung.

Day 6: Yumthang Valley and Zero Point

Explore Yumthang Valley and Zero Point. Overnight stay in Lachung.

Day 7: Transfer to Gangtok

Transfer back to Gangtok. Leisure time at MG Marg and Lal Market in the evening (optional). Overnight stay in Gangtok.

Day 8: East Sikkim Exploration

Transfer to Zuluk with sightseeing, including Changu Lake, Baba Mandir, and Nathula. Overnight stay in Zuluk.

Day 9: Zuluk to Lava

Transfer to Lava for an overnight stay.

Day 10: Lava Sightseeing

Explore Lava, Lolegaon, Rishop, and Ramdhura. Overnight stay in Lava.

Day 11: Departure

After breakfast, drop off at NJP station or airport (Siliguri).
