Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Sightseeing

  • Pick-up from NJP/IXB/Siliguri
  • Transfer to Rongo with en-route sightseeing:
    • Murti: A tranquil riverside destination perfect for relaxation and bird watching.
    • Jhalong: A beautiful hamlet situated on the banks of the Jaldhaka River, known for its scenic views and dam.
    • Bindu: The last village on the Indian side near Bhutan, surrounded by mountains and dense forests.
    • Laliguras: A charming spot for nature lovers, known for its vibrant blooms.
    • Rocky Island: A rocky riverbed destination ideal for adventure activities like rock climbing.
    • Suntalekhola: A picturesque location known for its tea gardens and lush surroundings.
  • Overnight stay at Rongo, a peaceful village on the Bhutan border in the Kalimpong District.

Day 2: Rongo 7-Point Sightseeing Tour

  • Explore the unique attractions of Rongo:
    • Rongo Zero Point: A scenic viewpoint offering panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys.
    • Rongo Old Buddha Monastery: A peaceful monastery with historical significance, perfect for spiritual reflection.
    • Dalgaon View Point: Offers breathtaking views of the Dooars region.
    • Jaldhaka River: A pristine river, providing an ideal spot for photography and nature walks.
    • Selfie Bridge: A picturesque location perfect for capturing stunning photographs.
    • Garisibas View Point: Another viewpoint offering sweeping views of the Bhutan border and surrounding landscapes.
    • Jholong Titanic Point & Bindu Drama: Scenic spots for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers.
  • Overnight stay at Rongo.

Day 3: Departure & Return

  • After breakfast, check out from Rongo and begin the return journey to NJP/IXB/Siliguri.
  • En-route sightseeing to explore any missed attractions.
