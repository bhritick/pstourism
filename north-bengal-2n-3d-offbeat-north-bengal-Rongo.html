2 Nights, 3 Days Offbeat North Bengal Trip

PST Holidayz with Pather Sandhane Tourism

An ISO 9001:2015 Certified Travel Organisation

Book Now

Pick-Up & Drop Locations: NJP (New Jalpaiguri Railway Station) / IXB (Bagdogra Airport) / Siliguri (SLG)

Destination: Rongo - Offbeat Kalimpong near Bhutan Border

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Sightseeing

Pick-Up: Pickup from NJP, IXB, or Siliguri and transfer to Rongo.

Sightseeing: Visit Murti, Jhalang, Bindu, Laliguras, Rocky Island, Suntalikhola.

Overnight Stay: Enjoy a relaxing stay at Rongo, an offbeat village near the Bhutan border in Kalimpong District.

Day 2: Rongo 7 Point Sightseeing

Explore major attractions: Rongo Zero Point, Old Buddha Monastery, Dalgaon View Point, Jaldhaka River, Selfie Bridge, Garisibas View Point, Jholong Titanic Point, Bindu Drama.

Overnight Stay: Stay another night at Rongo.

Day 3: Departure

Check-Out: After breakfast, check out and transfer to NJP with sightseeing on the way.

Inclusions

  • All quality meals (from Day 1 lunch to check-out day breakfast).
  • Quality lodging at Rongo.
  • Sightseeing transfers in an 8-seater Sumo/Bolero.
  • Driver allowance and experienced hill drivers.

Exclusions

  • Any entry fees, tickets, or parking charges.
  • Extra costs due to sudden closures or route changes caused by local or natural issues.
  • Anything not mentioned in the package.
