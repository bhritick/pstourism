Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Transfer to Pelling

Pickup from NJP Railway Station, IXB Airport, or Siliguri (SLG). Enjoy a scenic drive to Pelling and check-in at the hotel. Overnight stay at Pelling.

Day 2: Explore Yuksam

Day 3: Pelling to Ravangla with Glass Skywalk Tour

Transfer from Pelling to Ravangla. Enjoy the breathtaking view from the Glass Skywalk and visit the ancient Pemayangtse Monastery. Overnight stay at Ravangla.

Day 4: Ravangla Sightseeing

Visit the stunning Buddha Park, explore the Ralong Monastery, and stop at the Titanic View Point. Overnight stay at Ravangla.

Day 5: Transfer to Gangtok

On your way to Gangtok, visit Namchi Chardham, Temi Tea Garden, and the Samdruptse Statue. Overnight stay at Gangtok.

Day 6: Departure

After breakfast, drop-off at NJP Railway Station, IXB Airport, or Siliguri (SLG).
