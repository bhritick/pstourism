Offbeat North Bengal Trip - 2 Nights, 3 Days

Explore Sittong, Darjeeling, and Mirik with PST Holidayz

Day 1 - Arrival at NJP / Siliguri and Transfer to Sittong

Upon arrival at NJP or Siliguri, you will be transferred to the serene village of Sittong. On the way, visit:

  • Namthing Pokhri
  • Mahaldhiram Tea Garden
  • Ahaldhara Viewpoint

Overnight stay in Sittong.

Day 2 - Sittong to Darjeeling

After breakfast, drive to Darjeeling, with stops for sightseeing:

  • Yogi Ghat Bridge
  • Rabindra Museum
  • Lamahata Eco Park
  • Takdah Tinchule Viewpoint
  • Batasia Loop

Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 3 - Departure via Mirik

On your way back to NJP / Siliguri / Bagdogra Airport, visit:

  • Mirik Lake
  • Pashupati Market (Nepal Border)
  • Simana Viewpoint
  • Bokar Monastery

Return to NJP / Siliguri / Airport for departure.

Inclusions

  • All meals (from Day 1 lunch to Day 3 breakfast)
  • Comfortable lodging at Sittong and Darjeeling
  • Sightseeing transfers in a private cab with an experienced driver
  • Driver's allowance and permits

Exclusions

  • Entry fees or tickets at sightseeing spots
  • Personal expenses not mentioned in the itinerary
