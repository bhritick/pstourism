Tour Package: 7 Nights 8 Days - "Unforgettable Ladakh"

Hotel/Tent categories: Standard, Deluxe, and 3 Star

Tour Itinerary

  • Day 01: Arrival at Srinagar - Acclimatization, leisure, and final preparations. Overnight stay at Srinagar.
  • Day 02: Srinagar to Kargil via Sonmarg. Visit Namika-la, Kargil war museum, Zoji'la, and more. Overnight at Kargil.
  • Day 03: Kargil to Leh - Visit Lamayuru and Alchi monastery. Overnight stay at Shey/Leh.
  • Day 04: Leh local sightseeing - Gurudwara Pathar Sahib, Leh Palace, Shanti Stupa, and Leh market. Overnight at Shey/Leh.
  • Day 05: Shey/Leh to Hunder via Khardungla Pass. Overnight stay at Homestay/Tent/Hotel in Hunder/Deskit.
  • Day 06: Hunder/Deskit to Pangong Tso - Visit 3 Idiots Point and Pangong Lake. Overnight stay at Pangong Tent/Camp.
  • Day 07: Pangong Tso to Shey/Leh via Chang-la Pass. Overnight stay at Shey/Leh hotel.
  • Day 08: Departure from Shey/Leh - Transfer to the airport for your onward journey.

Inclusions

  • Hotel/Tent accommodation on a double-sharing basis.
  • Meal plan: Breakfast & Dinner (MAP).
  • Cab service for the entire itinerary.
  • Driver's food & accommodation.
  • Complimentary welcome drinks.

Exclusions

  • Personal expenses.
  • Flight tickets.
  • Lunch.
