Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Srinagar

Local sightseeing: Mughal Gardens, Dal Lake, Shankaracharya Temple. Overnight stay at a hotel in Srinagar.

Day 2: Full-day excursion to Sonmarg

Visit Thajiwas Glacier and enjoy breathtaking landscapes. Return to Srinagar in the evening. Overnight stay at a hotel in Srinagar.

Day 3: Drive from Srinagar to Pahalgam

Visit Baisaran Valley, referred to as "Mini Switzerland". Overnight stay at a hotel in Pahalgam.

Day 4: Explore local attractions in Pahalgam

Visit Aru Valley, Betaab Valley, and Chandanwari. Overnight stay at a hotel in Pahalgam.

Day 5: Travel from Pahalgam to Gulmarg

Enjoy activities like Gondola rides, trekking, and exploring the beautiful meadows of Gulmarg. Overnight stay at a hotel in Gulmarg.

Day 6: Return to Srinagar from Gulmarg

Spend the rest of the day at leisure or explore local markets in Srinagar. Overnight stay at a hotel in Srinagar.

Important Information

  • Pre-paid SIM cards don't work in J&K; only postpaid connections are functional.
  • Local cab required for Aru Valley, Betaab Valley & Chandanwari in Pahalgam.
  • Chained vehicles are required during snow in Gulmarg (Tangmarg to Gulmarg: 13 kms).

Accommodation Details

  • Srinagar: 8 nights at a hotel
  • Gulmarg: 1 night at a hotel
  • Pahalgam: 2 nights at a hotel
  • Houseboat stay: 1 night in Srinagar
  • Gurez Valley Camping: 2 nights
