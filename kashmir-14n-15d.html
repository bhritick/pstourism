14 Nights 15 Days Kashmir Trip

PST Holidayz by Pather Sandhane Tourism

Certified by: ISO 9001:2015 travel organisation

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Srinagar

  • Local sightseeing: Mughal Gardens, Dal Lake, Shankaracharya Temple.
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Srinagar.

Day 2: Full-day excursion to Sonmarg

  • Visit Thajiwas Glacier and enjoy breathtaking landscapes.
  • Return to Srinagar in the evening.
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Srinagar.

Day 3: Drive from Srinagar to Pahalgam

  • Visit Baisaran Valley, referred to as "Mini Switzerland".
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Pahalgam.

Day 4: Explore local attractions in Pahalgam

  • Visit Aru Valley, Betaab Valley, and Chandanwari.
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Pahalgam.

Day 5: Travel from Pahalgam to Gulmarg

  • Enjoy activities like Gondola rides, trekking, and exploring the beautiful meadows of Gulmarg.
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Gulmarg.

Day 6: Return to Srinagar from Gulmarg

  • Spend the rest of the day at leisure or explore local markets in Srinagar.
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Srinagar.

Day 7: Day trip to Doodhpathri

  • Visit the serene Doodhpathri meadows with lush green grasslands and a river flowing through.
  • Return to Srinagar in the evening.
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Srinagar.

Day 8: Full-day excursion to Verinag and Kokernag

  • Visit Verinag, the source of the Jhelum River, and Kokernag known for its gardens and springs.
  • Return to Srinagar in the evening.
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Srinagar.

Day 9: Day trip to Aharbal

  • Visit Aharbal, famous for its beautiful waterfall and surrounding pine forests.
  • Return to Srinagar in the evening.
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Srinagar.

Day 10: Excursion to Bangus Valley

  • Explore Bangus Valley, a hidden gem known for its scenic beauty and meadows.
  • Return to Srinagar in the evening.
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Srinagar.

Day 11: Day trip to Yousmarg

  • Visit Yousmarg, a tranquil meadow surrounded by dense forests and snow-capped mountains.
  • Return to Srinagar and enjoy an overnight stay at a houseboat.

Day 12: Travel from Srinagar to Gurez Valley

  • Enjoy the untouched beauty and pristine landscapes of Gurez Valley.
  • Overnight camping in Gurez.

Day 13: Explore Gurez Valley

  • Visit Chakwali Village, a charming spot surrounded by mountains.
  • Overnight camping in Gurez.

Day 14: Return from Gurez Valley to Srinagar

  • Spend the rest of the day at leisure or explore Srinagar's local attractions and markets.
  • Overnight stay at a houseboat in Srinagar.

Day 15: Drop off at Srinagar Airport

  • End of the tour. Departure from Srinagar Airport.

Accommodation Details

  • Srinagar: 8 nights at a hotel
  • Gulmarg: 1 night at a hotel
  • Pahalgam: 2 nights at a hotel
  • Houseboat stay: 1 night in Srinagar
  • Gurez Valley Camping: 2 nights

Inclusions

  • Hotel Category: Standard/Deluxe/Premium
  • Meals: MAPAI (Breakfast, Dinner & Taxes)
  • Cab for all sightseeing, Pickup & Drop Transfers
  • 1-hour Shikara Ride on Dal Lake
  • Experienced Driver and Necessary Permit

Exclusions

  • Air/Train fare
  • Meals other than those mentioned in inclusions
  • Personal expenses (tips, phone calls, laundry, liquor, etc.)
  • Entrance fees, Cable Car/Pony Ride costs

Important Information

  • Pre-paid SIM cards don't work in J&K; only postpaid connections are functional.
  • Local cab required for Aru Valley, Betaab Valley & Chandanwari in Pahalgam.
  • Chained vehicles are required during snow in Gulmarg (Tangmarg to Gulmarg: 13 kms).

Cancellation Policy

  • Up to 15 days before departure: 45% of the package cost will be deducted.
  • Between 8-14 days: 70% of the package cost will be deducted.
  • Between 4-7 days: 95% of the package cost will be deducted.
  • Less than 3 days: 100% of the package cost will be deducted.
