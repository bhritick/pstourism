4 Nights 5 Days Andaman Trip

Discover the beauty of Andaman with Pather Sandhane Tourism (ISO 9001:2015 certified)

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Port Blair City Tour

Arrive at Port Blair airport and transfer to the hotel. After lunch, visit Cellular Jail and Corbyn's Cove Beach. In the evening, enjoy the Light & Sound show at Cellular Jail. Overnight stay in Port Blair.

Day 2: Port Blair to Havelock Island

After breakfast, take the Green Ocean Cruise/Sea Link to Havelock Island. Enjoy the famous Radhanagar Beach, one of Asia's top beaches. Overnight stay in Havelock.

Day 3: Havelock to Neil Island

Visit Kalapathar Beach for a spectacular sunrise. Later, transfer to Neil Island to explore Bharatpur Beach, Laxmanpur Beach, and the Natural Bridge. Overnight stay in Neil Island.

Day 4: Neil Island to Port Blair

After breakfast, return to Port Blair by ferry. Enjoy the rest of the day at leisure. Overnight stay in Port Blair.

Day 5: Departure

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed to Port Blair Airport for your departure, carrying happy memories of Andaman.
