Overview of the Standard Property Package

This 3-day, 2-night tour offers a perfect blend of adventure and relaxation. It's an ideal choice for those who want to experience the wonders of Sundarban without breaking the bank. Our package includes comfortable lodgings, delicious meals, and thrilling activities, all while keeping the costs low.

Book Now

Package Inclusions:

  • Transportation: Pick-up and drop-off service from Canning Railway Station.
  • Accommodation: Standard lodgings with options for AC and Non-AC rooms.
  • Meals: All major meals including breakfast, lunch, tea, snacks, and dinner.
  • Activities: Guided boat rides, jungle safaris, and cultural programs.
  • Permits: Jungle entry fees, boat permits, and still camera permissions.

Package Exclusions:

  • Hard or aerated drinks
  • Video camera charges
  • Miscellaneous expenses
  • Coolie charges
  • Permit fees for non-Indian guests

Tour Details

  • Kolkata To Kolkata
  • Canning TO Canning
  • Godkhali To Godkhali

Day 1: Arrival and Exploration

"Pickup from Canning/Godkhali and transfer to Pakhiralaya, with a sightseeing tour of villages along the river in Sundarbans. Overnight stay at Pakhiralaya Hotel or Cottage.

Day 2: Jungle Safari and River Exploration

"Begin your jungle journey early in the morning with visits to Sajnekhali National Park, Netidhopani, and Dobanki Watch Towers to experience the diverse wildlife of Sundarbans. Enjoy a delicious breakfast and lunch served on the safari boat. In the evening, immerse yourself in the cultural experience of a traditional tribal dance program at the hotel. Overnight stay at Pakhiralaya Cottage.

Day 3: Cultural Exploration and Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a morning walk along the banks of the Matla River. Following this, we will begin our return journey, including a visit to Gosaba for local sightseeing. Explore village life, the local market, Rabindranath Tagore’s Beacon Bungalow, and Hamilton Bungalow. We will conclude the tour with lunch before dropping you off at Canning or Godkhali.

Kolkata to Gadkhali Route: Sealdah to Canning by train (1.5 hrs) or by road via Baruipur, or we will arrange. Canning to Gadkhali by Auto/Magic/Bus(30 min) arrange accordingly Package
