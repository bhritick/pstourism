PST HolidayZ by Pather Sandhane Tourism

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Pickup from NJP and transfer to Sittong

Sightseeing: Namching Pokri, Mahaldhiram Tea Garden, Ahaldhara View Point. Overnight stay at Sittong.

Day 2: Sittong to Tukdah

Sightseeing: Yogighat Bridge, Mongpo Rabindra Bhawan. Overnight stay at Tukdah.

Day 3: Transfer from Tukdah to Darjeeling

Sightseeing: Lamahata Eco Park, Batasia Loop, Ghoom Monastery. Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 4: Darjeeling sightseeing

Sightseeing: Tiger Hill, Peace Pagoda, Japanese Temple, Tenzing Rock, Rangeet Valley Tea Garden, Ropeway, HMI. Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 5: Check-out and transfer to NJP

Sightseeing: Mirik Lake, Pasupati Market (Nepal border), Bokar Monastery, Simana View Point, Gopaldhara Tea Garden. Return to NJP.
