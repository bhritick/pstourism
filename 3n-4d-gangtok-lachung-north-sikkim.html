Trip Itinerary

Day 1: Transfer to Gangtok

Pickup from NJP Station or Airport and transfer to Gangtok. Overnight stay in Gangtok.

Day 2: North Sikkim Exploration

Transfer to Lachen with sightseeing along the way. Overnight stay in Lachen.

Day 3: Explore Gurudongmar Lake & Transfer to Lachung

Morning visit to Gurudongmar Lake, followed by transfer to Lachung. Overnight stay in Lachung.

Day 4: Explore Zero Point & Transfer to Gangtok

Visit Zero Point and return to Gangtok with some sightseeing along the way. Overnight stay in Gangtok.

Day 5 (4 Nights 5 Days Package): Drop-off at NJP or Airport

After breakfast, transfer to NJP or Bagdogra Airport with a few stops for sightseeing.

Day 5 (5 Nights 6 Days Package): Visit Tosamo Lake, Baba Mandir, and Nathula Pass

Sightseeing of Tosamo Lake, Baba Mandir, and Nathula Pass. Overnight stay in Gangtok.

Day 6 (5 Nights 6 Days Package): Drop-off at NJP or Airport

After breakfast, transfer to NJP Station or Bagdogra Airport.
