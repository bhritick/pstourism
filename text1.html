South West Sikkim Tour Package - 6 Days 5 Nights

Explore the serene beauty of Pelling, Yuksam, Ravangla, and Gangtok with Pather Sandhane Tourism (ISO 9001:2015 certified).

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: NJP/IXB/SLG Pickup & Transfer to Pelling

Our journey begins with a pickup from NJP railway station, IXB airport, or Siliguri. We head to Pelling in West Sikkim for an overnight stay.

Day 2: Explore Yuksam - The Oldest Capital of Sikkim

Visit Yuksam, the first capital of Sikkim, and explore Yuksam Lake, Rimbi Orange Garden, Rimbi River, Thangtong Gyalpo Statue, King's Palace, and more. Overnight stay in Pelling.

Day 3: Pelling to Ravangla with Glass Skywalk Visit

Transfer from Pelling to Ravangla with a stop at the Glass Skywalk and the nearby oldest monastery. Overnight stay in Ravangla.

Day 4: Ravangla Sightseeing

Spend the day sightseeing in Ravangla, including Buddha Park, Ralong Monastery, Titanic View Point, and more. Overnight stay in Ravangla.

Day 5: Ravangla to Gangtok

On Day 5, transfer to Gangtok while visiting Namchi Chardham, Temi Tea Garden, and Samdruptse en route. Overnight stay in Gangtok.

Day 6: Departure

After breakfast, check out and drop off at NJP railway station, IXB airport, or Siliguri. End of the 6-day tour.
