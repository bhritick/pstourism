6 Days South West Sikkim Package Trip

Organized by PST Holidayz

Discover the stunning landscapes of South West Sikkim with our 6-day package. Pather Sandhane Tourism, an ISO 9001:2015 certified travel organization, offers a comprehensive tour including Pelling, Yuksam, Ravangla, and Gangtok. Experience the best of Sikkim with quality lodging and expert guidance.

Tour Itinerary

  • Day 1: Pickup from NJP/IXB/SLG and transfer to Pelling, West Sikkim. Overnight stay at Pelling.
  • Day 2: Explore Yuksam, the oldest capital of Sikkim. Sightseeing includes Yuksam Lake, Rimbi Orange Garden, Rimbi River, Thangtong Gyalpo Statue, King's Palace, and more. Overnight stay at Pelling.
  • Day 3: Transfer from Pelling to Ravangla with sightseeing at the Glass Skywalk and the oldest monastery nearby. Overnight stay at Ravangla.
  • Day 4: Ravangla sightseeing, including Buddha Park, Ralong Monastery, Titanic View Point, and more. Overnight stay at Ravangla.
  • Day 5: Transfer to Gangtok with sightseeing at Namchi Chardham, Temi Tea Garden, and Samdruptse. Overnight stay at Gangtok.
  • Day 6: After breakfast, drop-off at NJP station/IXB/SLG.
