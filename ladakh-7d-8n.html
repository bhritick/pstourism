Tour Itinerary

Day 01: Arrival at Srinagar

Our trip starts when you arrive in Srinagar, the summer capital of Jammu and Kashmir. This day is for acclimatization, leisure, and final presentations for the trip. Rest well. Overnight stay at Srinagar.

Day 02: Srinagar to Kargil via Sonmarg

After breakfast, check out from Srinagar hotel and drive to Kargil via Sonmarg. Visit Namika-la (the highest pass on Leh-Srinagar road), Lamayuru monastery, Kargil War Museum, and Zoji'la Pass. Overnight stay at Kargil.

Day 03: Kargil to Leh

After breakfast, drive to Shey/Leh. Visit Lamayuru monastery, Alchi monastery (famous for 1000-year-old frescoes), and Drass on the way. Overnight stay in Shey/Leh.

Day 04: Leh Local Sightseeing

Explore the vibrant capital of Ladakh, visiting Gurudwara Pathar Sahib, Leh Palace, Shey Palace, Shanti Stupa, and the Leh Ladakh Market. Overnight stay in Shey/Leh.

Day 05: Shey/Leh to Hundar via Khardungla Pass

After breakfast, check out of the hotel and start your journey to Nubra Valley through Khardungla Pass. Overnight stay at our homestay/tent/hotel in Hunder/Deskit.

Day 06: Hunder/Deskit to Pangong Tso

Today, we start early for the most awaited part of the trip: the beautiful Pangong Lake. Visit 3 Idiots Point and the Pangong Tso Lake. Overnight stay at Pangong tent/camp.

Day 07: Pangong Tso to Shey/Leh via Chang-la Pass

Visit the Changla Baba Temple on top of Chang-la Pass. Overnight stay at Shey/Leh.

Day 08: Departure from Shey/Leh

After breakfast, we will drop you at the airport. Say goodbye to fellow travelers and this magical land.
