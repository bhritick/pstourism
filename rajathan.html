4 Nights, 5 Days Rajasthan Package Trip

Explore the beauty of Rajasthan with Pather Sandhane Tourism, an ISO 9001:2015 certified travel organization

Day 1 - Jaipur Sightseeing

Pickup from Jaipur Airport in the morning and visit the following attractions:

  • Amer Fort (1 hour)
  • Jal Mahal (10 minutes)
  • Hawa Mahal (view on the way)
  • City Palace + Jantar Mantar (2 hours)

Overnight stay in Jaipur.

Day 2 - Jaipur to Jodhpur

Early morning checkout from Jaipur and transfer to Jodhpur. Visit:

  • Mehrangarh Fort (30 minutes)
  • Jaswant Thada (30 minutes)
  • Umaid Bhawan Palace (2 hours)

Overnight stay in Jaisalmer.

Day 3 - Jaisalmer Sightseeing & Desert Camp

Explore Jaisalmer by visiting:

  • Sonar Kella (Jaisalmer Fort)
  • Patwon Ki Haveli
  • Gadisar Lake

Check-in to a desert camp for a night stay. Enjoy a cultural dance program, camel ride, and jeep safari (jeep safari at extra cost).

Day 4 - Jaisalmer to Mount Abu

Checkout from the desert camp early at 4 AM and head to Mount Abu. Visit:

  • Dilwara Jain Temple
  • Om Shanti Bhawan
  • Nakki Lake (in the evening)

Overnight stay in Mount Abu.

Day 5 - Departure from Mount Abu

Early morning checkout from Mount Abu and transfer back to Jaipur Airport (12 hours drive). Drop off at the airport.
