Puri Holiday Package

Explore the spiritual and scenic beauty of Puri with Pather Sandhane Tourism (ISO 9001:2015 certified).

Book Now

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Sightseeing

Morning pick-up from Puri Bus Stand or Railway Station. Explore:

  • Konark Sun Temple (UNESCO World Heritage Site)
  • Nandankanan Zoological Park
  • Dhabalgiri Buddhist Site

Overnight stay at Puri.

Day 2: Jagannath Temple and Chilika Lake

Start the day with a visit to the famous Jagannath Temple for Mahaprabhu Puja, followed by a trip to:

  • Chilika Lake – Enjoy a boat ride and explore Asia’s largest coastal lagoon.

Overnight stay at Puri.

Day 3: Final Sightseeing and Departure

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and enjoy sightseeing of:

  • Puri Sea Beach
  • ISKCON Temple
  • Khandagiri and Udayagiri Caves
  • Chandrabhaga Beach
  • Sand Sculpture Museum
  • Sonar Gouranga Temple

Drop-off at Puri Bus Stand or Railway Station for departure.

Package Inclusions & Exclusions

Inclusions:

  • Quality lodging throughout the tour
  • Daily breakfast
  • Sightseeing as per the itinerary
  • Pick-up and drop-off services

Exclusions:

  • Entry fees and parking charges
  • Puja arrangements at Jagannath Temple
  • Any personal expenses
