Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at NJP/IXB & Transfer to Pelling

  • Pick-up: From NJP Railway Station, IXB Airport, or Siliguri.
  • Drive: Scenic journey to Pelling (approx. 6-7 hours).
  • Check-in: At your Pelling accommodation and relax after your journey.
  • Overnight Stay: Pelling.

Day 2: Explore Pelling

  • After Breakfast: Visit Pelling's famous attractions:
  • Bird Park: Home to local and exotic birds in a serene environment.
  • Highest Glass Skywalk: Experience stunning views of Mt. Kanchenjunga and valleys.
  • Pemayangste Monastery: A peaceful and historical monastery.
  • Kechupalri Lake: Sacred and tranquil, surrounded by dense forests.
  • Kanchenjunga Waterfalls: Stunning natural beauty with cascading clear waters.
  • Darap Village: Explore Sikkim's authentic local culture.
  • Overnight Stay: Pelling.

Day 3: Discover Yuksam

  • After Breakfast: Drive to Yuksam, the first capital of Sikkim.
  • Visit: Yuksam Lake, a serene spot surrounded by natural beauty.
  • Rimbi Orange Garden: Learn about local fruit cultivation.
  • Thangtong Gyalpo Statue: Pay respects to this revered Buddhist saint.
  • King Palace: Historical residence of Sikkim royalty.
  • Return: To Pelling for an overnight stay.

Day 4: Transfer to Kaluk - Offbeat Gem of West Sikkim

  • After Breakfast: Drive to Kaluk, a hidden gem of West Sikkim.
  • Check-in: At your Kaluk accommodation and relax.
  • Explore: The local area and its natural beauty.
  • Overnight Stay: Kaluk.

Day 5: Departure from Kaluk to NJP/IXB

  • After Breakfast: Check-out and proceed back to NJP Railway Station, IXB Airport, or Siliguri.
  • Depart: With wonderful memories of your West Sikkim Tour!
