5-Night 6-Day Special East-South-West Sikkim Package

Destinations: Gangtok - Ravangla - Pelling

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Gangtok

  • Pickup: Transfer from NJP / IXB / Siliguri to Gangtok

  • Overnight Stay: Gangtok

Day 2: Transfer to Ravangla with Sightseeing

  • Morning: Depart from Gangtok to Ravangla
  • En Route Sightseeing: Visit Namchi Chardham, Temi Tea Garden, and Sandruptse
  • Overnight Stay: Ravangla

Day 3: Ravangla Sightseeing

  • Sightseeing: Buddha Park, Ralong Monastery, and Titenic View Point
  • Overnight Stay: Ravangla

Day 4: Transfer to Pelling with Sightseeing

  • Morning: Transfer from Ravangla to Pelling
  • Sightseeing: Enjoy the Glass Sky Walk and visit the Oldest Monastery in Pelling
  • Overnight Stay: Pelling

Day 5: Relaxation Day in Pelling

  • Activities: Explore the local market and savor local cuisine
  • Overnight Stay: Pelling

Day 6: Departure

  • Morning: Breakfast and transfer to NJP for departure
