5-Night 6-Day Special East-South-West Sikkim Package

Gangtok - Ravangla - Pelling

Book Now

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Transfer to Gangtok

Pickup Locations: NJP Railway Station, IXB Airport, or Siliguri

Enjoy the evening exploring MG Marg and Lal Market.

Accommodation: Overnight stay at a hotel in Gangtok

Day 2: Transfer to Ravangla with Sightseeing

On the way to Ravangla, visit:

  • Namchi Chardham: A famous pilgrimage spot with replicas of sacred Hindu shrines.
  • Temi Tea Garden: The only tea estate in Sikkim offering stunning landscapes.
  • Samdruptse: A hill with a towering statue of Guru Padmasambhava.

Accommodation: Overnight stay at a hotel in Ravangla

Day 3: Ravangla Sightseeing

  • Buddha Park: A serene park with a giant statue of Buddha offering panoramic views.
  • Ralong Monastery: One of the oldest monasteries in Sikkim, rich in history and culture.
  • Titanic View Point: A scenic viewpoint offering majestic views of the surroundings.

Accommodation: Overnight stay at a hotel in Ravangla

Day 4: Transfer to Pelling with Sightseeing

  • Glass Sky Walk: Experience breathtaking views from the highest glass sky walk in Sikkim.
  • Oldest Monastery in Pelling: Visit the historic Pemayangtse Monastery.

Accommodation: Overnight stay at a hotel in Pelling

Day 5: Relax and Explore Local Markets in Pelling

Spend a leisurely day exploring Pelling's local markets and savoring authentic local food.

Accommodation: Overnight stay at a hotel in Pelling

Day 6: Departure

After breakfast, we will drop you at NJP Railway Station, IXB Airport, or your chosen departure point.

Inclusions

  • Quality accommodation at all destinations as per the itinerary
  • Authentic meals from Day 1 lunch to breakfast on the day of checkout
  • Pickup, drop, and sightseeing transfers in a cab (shared or personal, as per package)
  • Necessary permits and driver allowances

Exclusions

  • Entry fees and tickets not mentioned in the itinerary
  • Parking fees, food during travel, or additional charges due to changes in the route
  • Local cab costs
  • No refunds for missed sightseeing points due to unforeseen issues
