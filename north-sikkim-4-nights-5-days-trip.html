Optional Add-Ons (Extra Charges Apply)

Zero Point

The last accessible point near the Indo-China border, known for year-round snowfall.

Kala Pattharr

A unique black rocky terrain with mesmerizing high-altitude views.

Mt. Katao

Often called “Sikkim’s Switzerland,” this offbeat destination offers breathtaking snowfall and adventure sports like skiing (seasonal).

East Sikkim Highlights (5N/6D Package Only)

Nathula Pass

A historic Indo-China border pass at 14,140 ft, accessible with a special permit (subject to availability).

Tsomgo Lake (Changu Lake)

A stunning glacial lake at 12,400 ft, known for its seasonal transformations from crystal-clear waters to a frozen paradise.

Baba Mandir

A temple dedicated to Baba Harbhajan Singh, believed to be the guardian spirit of the region.
