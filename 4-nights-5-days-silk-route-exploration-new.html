Day-Wise Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Serenity at Sillerygaon/Ramdhura

  • Welcome to the Gateway of the Himalayas! Upon arrival at NJP Railway Station/Bagdogra Airport, you'll be greeted by our team.
  • Enjoy a scenic drive to Sillerygaon or Ramdhura, serene hamlets amidst the lush greenery of the Eastern Himalayas.
  • Sillerygaon: Known as the “New Darjeeling,” offers breathtaking views of Mt. Kanchenjunga and unmatched tranquility.
  • Ramdhura: A paradise of misty clouds and verdant landscapes, perfect for nature lovers and photographers.
  • En route, marvel at the serene Reshi River or Rongpo River, perfect for a quick photo stop or enjoying the soothing sounds of nature.
  • Check-in at your cozy homestay, enjoy traditional hospitality, and relax.
  • Overnight Stay: Sillerygaon/Ramdhura.

Day 2: Riverside Bliss at Rishikhola/Rorathang

  • After a traditional breakfast, head to Rishikhola or Rorathang, famous for their riverside charm.
  • Rishikhola: A serene retreat by the Reshi River, surrounded by forests and wildlife.
  • Rorathang: A lesser-explored gem with scenic views and vibrant local culture.
  • En route, visit Rangoli Market and Siddhivinayak Mandir.
  • Spend the evening strolling by the riverside or unwinding amidst nature.
  • Overnight Stay: Rishikhola/Rorathang.

Day 3: Mesmerizing Silk Route Highlights - Phadamchen/Zuluk

  • Begin your journey early to Phadamchen or Zuluk, steeped in history and natural beauty.
  • Highlights:
  • Quekhola Falls
  • Zigzag Road with 32 hairpin bends
  • Thambi Viewpoint
  • Nathang Valley
  • Overnight Stay: Phadamchen/Zuluk.

Day 4: Exploring Alpine Wonders & Transfer to Gangtok

  • Explore alpine attractions such as Zuluk Viewpoint and Elephant Lake/Kupup Lake.
  • Visit New & Old Baba Mandir and Changu Lake (Tsomgo Lake).
  • Transfer to Gangtok and enjoy MG Marg in the evening.
  • Overnight Stay: Gangtok.

Day 5: Farewell with Cherished Memories

  • After breakfast, bid farewell to the Eastern Himalayas as we transfer you back to NJP Railway Station/Bagdogra Airport.
  • Leave with a heart full of memories, stunning photos, and the promise of returning to explore more with PSTourism.
