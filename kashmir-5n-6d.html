Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Srinagar

  • Local sightseeing in Srinagar, including the Mughal Gardens, Dal Lake, and Shankaracharya Temple.
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Srinagar.

Day 2: Full-day excursion to Sonmarg

  • Visit Thajiwas Glacier and enjoy the breathtaking landscapes.
  • Return to Srinagar in the evening.
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Srinagar.

Day 3: Srinagar to Pahalgam

  • Drive to Pahalgam, a picturesque town set amidst the Lidder River.
  • Visit Baisaran Valley, often referred to as "Mini Switzerland."
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Pahalgam.

Day 4: Pahalgam Exploration

  • Visit Aru Valley, Betaab Valley, and Chandanwari.
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Pahalgam.

Inclusions

  • Hotel Category: Standard/Deluxe/Premium
  • MAPAI (Breakfast, Dinner & Taxes)
  • Cab for entire sightseeing, Pickup, Drop Transfer
  • Shikara Ride on Dal Lake (01 Hour)
  • Experienced Driver, Necessary Permits

Exclusions

  • Airfare/Train fare
  • Meals other than those specified
  • Personal expenses (tips, phone calls, laundry, etc.)
  • Guide and Entrance fees during sightseeing
  • Cable Car/Pony Ride in Gulmarg, Pahalgam & Sonmarg

Important Information

  • Prepaid SIM cards don't work in J&K. Only postpaid connections are allowed.
  • Bargain to your max for activities like Pony/Horse rides, Sledging, Rafting, etc.
  • Local cabs are required to visit Aru Valley, Betaab Valley, and Chandanwari (Approx. Rs. 1800 - 2000 per vehicle).
  • During snow in Gulmarg, chained vehicles are required (Tangmarg to Gulmarg - 13 km).

Cancellation Policy

  • Up to 15 days before departure: 45% of package cost
  • 8-14 days before departure: 70% of package cost
  • 4-7 days before departure: 95% of package cost
  • Less than 3 days: 100% of package cost
