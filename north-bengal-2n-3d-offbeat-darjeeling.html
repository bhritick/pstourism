2 Nights, 3 Days North Bengal Trip

PST Holidayz with Pather Sandhane Tourism

An ISO 9001:2015 Certified Travel Organisation

Book Now

Pick-Up & Drop Locations: NJP (New Jalpaiguri Railway Station) / IXB (Bagdogra Airport) / Siliguri (SLG)

Destinations: Sittong - Tukdah - Tinchule - Lamahata

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Local Sightseeing

Pick-Up: Pickup from NJP, IXB, or Siliguri and transfer to Sittong.

Sightseeing: Visit Namching Pokri, Mahaldhiram Tea Garden, and Ahaldhara View Point.

Overnight Stay: Stay overnight at Sittong.

Day 2: Sittong to Tukdah

Sightseeing: Explore Yogi Ghat Bridge, Rabindra Bhawan, Lamahata Eco Park, and Tinchule View Point.

Overnight Stay: Stay overnight at Tuldah / Tinchule / Lamahata.

Day 3: Departure

Check-Out: After breakfast, transfer to NJP with sightseeing at Sevak Coronation Bridge and Sevak Kali Bari.

Inclusions

  • All quality meals (from Day 1 lunch to check-out day breakfast).
  • Quality lodging (standard/deluxe/premium) at Sittong and Tuldah.
  • Pickup and sightseeing transfers in 4/8-seater cab.
  • Driver allowance and experienced hill drivers.

Exclusions

  • Any entry fees, tickets, or parking charges.
  • Food during the journey.
  • Anything not mentioned in the package.
