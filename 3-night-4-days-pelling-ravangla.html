Tour Itinerary

Join Pather Sandhane Tourism on this thrilling 4-day, 3-night journey through the serene beauty of West Sikkim, exploring Pelling, Ravangla, and beyond.

Day 1: Arrival and Transfer to Pelling

  • Pickup from NJP Station
  • Proceed to Pelling for your stay
  • Overnight stay at Pelling

Day 2: West Sikkim Exploration

  • Sightseeing at:
    • Rimbi River
    • Rimbi Orange Garden
    • Rimbi Waterfalls
    • Kanchanjunga Waterfalls
    • India's Highest Glass Sky Walk (7200 ft)
    • Sanghak Choeling Monastery
    • Khecheopalri Lake (Sacred to Hindus and Buddhists)
  • Overnight stay at Pelling

Day 3: Pelling to Ravangla via Namchi Chardham

  • Transfer from Pelling to Ravangla
  • Sightseeing at Namchi Chardham
  • Overnight stay at Ravangla

Day 4: Departure

  • After breakfast, we will drop you at NJP Station
