4 Days 3 Nights Sikkim Trip

Explore Pelling, Ravangla, and Namchi Chardham with Pather Sandhane Tourism (ISO 9001:2015 certified).

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Transfer to Pelling

Pick-up from NJP station, transfer to Pelling for an overnight stay.

Day 2: West Sikkim Exploration

Explore the scenic beauty of Rimbi River, Rimbi Orange Garden, Glass Sky Walk, and Khecheopalri Lake. Overnight stay in Pelling.

Day 3: Pelling to Ravangla via Namchi Chardham

Transfer from Pelling to Ravangla, stopping at Namchi Chardham along the way. Overnight stay in Ravangla.

Day 4: Departure

After breakfast, check out and transfer to NJP station.
