Tour Itinerary

  • Day 01: Pick up from Port Blair airport. Check-in to the hotel. Evening visit to Cellular Jail, Corbyn's Cove Beach & Light & Sound Show. Overnight stay at Port Blair.
  • Day 02: 2 Island Tour: Visit Rose Island and North Bay Island. Overnight stay at Port Blair.
  • Day 03: Travel from Port Blair to Havelock Island. Visit famous Radhanagar Beach. Overnight stay at Havelock Island.
  • Day 04: Travel from Havelock to Neil Island. Visit Bharatpur, Laxmanpur & Natural Bridge. Overnight stay at Neil Island.
  • Day 05: Return from Neil Island to Port Blair. Free day for leisure. Overnight stay at Port Blair.
  • Day 06: Drop off at Port Blair airport.
