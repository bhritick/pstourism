BHUTAN (4N 5D)

Meal Plan: MAP (Modified American Plan - Breakfast & Dinner)

Book Now

Itinerary

Day 1: Jaigaon - Phuentsholing to Thimphu

Begin your journey from Jaigaon/Phuentsholing and drive to Thimphu. Enjoy sightseeing along the way and reach Thimphu for an overnight stay.

Night Stay: Thimphu

Day 2: Thimphu to Punakha

After breakfast, head to Punakha, passing through the scenic Dochu-La Pass. Visit the iconic Punakha Dzong, cross the Suspension Bridge, and enjoy river rafting. Return to Thimphu for the night stay.

Night Stay: Thimphu

Day 3: Thimphu Sightseeing & Transfer to Paro

After breakfast, explore Thimphu’s cultural landmarks:

  • Buddha Point
  • BBS Tower
  • Takin National Zoo
  • Simply Bhutan Museum
  • Clock Tower
  • Kaja Throm
  • Hindus Temple

In the evening, transfer to Paro for a night stay.

Night Stay: Paro

Day 4: Paro Local Sightseeing

After breakfast, explore Paro’s famous landmarks:

  • Paro Town (International Airport Viewpoint)
  • Tiger's Nest Monastery (Trekking)
  • Nya Mey Zam Bridge
  • Zinpung Dzong
  • Kichu Lakhang

Night Stay: Paro

Day 5: Paro to Phuentsholing – Jaigaon Drop

After breakfast, drive back to Phuentsholing/Jaigaon, concluding your trip with unforgettable memories of Bhutan.

Package Details

Hotels: 3-Star Deluxe Rooms (with Breakfast & Dinner)

  • 2 Nights at Thimphu: Hotel White Tara / Hotel Darlha / Khamsum Inn or similar
  • 2 Nights at Paro: Hotel Chumo Choki Resort / Ratna Vara / Sangayling or similar

Inclusions

  • Accommodation in 3-star hotels (with breakfast and dinner)
  • Transportation (Jaigaon – Thimphu – Punakha – Paro – Jaigaon)
  • Sustainable Development Fee (SDF)
  • Travel Insurance
  • Guide Services

Exclusions

  • Flights/Train fare
  • Lunch (only breakfast & dinner included)
  • Entry fees for monuments
  • Personal expenses (shopping, tips, etc.)
  • Any additional sightseeing not mentioned in the itinerary
