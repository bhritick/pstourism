Explore Port Blair, Havelock, and Neil Island with Pather Sandhane Tourism (ISO 9001:2015 certified).

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Port Blair & City Tour

Our representative will pick you up from Port Blair airport and escort you to your hotel. In the evening, visit the historic Cellular Jail, Corbyn's Cove Beach, and enjoy the Light & Sound Show. Overnight stay at Port Blair hotel.

Day 2: Ross Island & North Bay Island Tour

Explore the remnants of colonial history at Ross Island, then head to North Bay Island for water sports and coral reef exploration. Overnight stay at Port Blair hotel.

Day 3: Port Blair to Havelock Island

Take a private cruise to Havelock Island. Visit the famous Radhanagar Beach, one of the best beaches in Asia. Overnight stay at Havelock Island hotel.

Day 4: Havelock to Neil Island

Travel to Neil Island and visit Bharatpur Beach, Laxmanpur Beach, and the Natural Bridge. Enjoy the peaceful surroundings and natural beauty. Overnight stay at Neil Island hotel.

Day 5: Neil Island to Port Blair

Return to Port Blair via cruise. Enjoy a free day to explore the city or relax. Overnight stay at Port Blair hotel.

Day 6: Departure

Check out and transfer to the airport for your return journey. Our representative will ensure a smooth drop-off at the Port Blair airport.
