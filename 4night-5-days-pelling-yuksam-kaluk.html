Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Transfer to Pelling

Destination: Pelling

Accommodation: Overnight stay at a hotel in Pelling

Day 2: Explore Pelling

  • Bird Park: Discover a diverse collection of local and exotic bird species.
  • Highest Glass Sky Walk: Enjoy breathtaking views from the highest glass sky walk in Sikkim.
  • Pemayangste Monastery: Visit this ancient monastery known for its serene atmosphere and architecture.
  • Kechuphelri Lake: A picturesque lake surrounded by lush greenery, perfect for a tranquil retreat.
  • Kanchanjungha Waterfalls: Marvel at the stunning waterfalls with views of the majestic Kanchenjunga.
  • Darap Village: Discover the local culture and lifestyle in this charming village.

Accommodation: Overnight stay at a hotel in Pelling
