4-Night 5-Day Special West Sikkim Offbeat Tour Package

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Transfer to Pelling

  • Pickup Locations: NJP Railway Station, IXB Airport, or Siliguri
  • Destination: Pelling
  • Accommodation: Overnight stay at a hotel in Pelling

Day 2: Explore Pelling

Bird Park: Enjoy a diverse collection of local and exotic bird species.

Highest Glass Sky Walk: Experience breathtaking views from the highest sky walk in Sikkim.

Pemayangste Monastery: Visit this ancient monastery known for its beautiful architecture and serene atmosphere.

Kechuphelri Lake: A picturesque lake surrounded by lush greenery, perfect for a tranquil retreat.

Kanchanjungha Water Falls: Marvel at the stunning waterfalls with views of the majestic Kanchenjunga.

Darap Village: Discover the local culture and lifestyle in this charming village.

Accommodation: Overnight stay at a hotel in Pelling

Day 3: Discover Yuksam

  • Yuksam: The historic first capital of Sikkim.
  • Yuksam Lake: A serene lake surrounded by natural beauty.
  • Rimbi Orange Garden: Visit this garden for a glimpse of local fruit cultivation.
  • Thangtong Gyalpo Statue: Pay your respects at this statue dedicated to the renowned Buddhist saint.
  • King Palace: Explore the historical residence of Sikkim’s royalty.

Accommodation: Return to Pelling for Overnight Stay

Day 4: Transfer to Kaluk - The Offbeat Gem of West Sikkim

  • Destination: Kaluk, an offbeat and tranquil location in West Sikkim.
  • Accommodation: Overnight stay at a hotel in Kaluk

Day 5: Departure

  • Transfer: Return journey to NJP Station or other departure points

Inclusions

  • Accommodation: Quality lodging as per the itinerary
  • Meals: Authentic local food from lunch on Day 1 to breakfast on the day of checkout
  • Transfers: Pickup, drop, and sightseeing transfers in a cab (shared or personal, as per package)
  • Permits: Necessary permissions and driver allowances

Exclusions

  • Entry Fees and Tickets: Any entry fees or tickets not mentioned in the itinerary
  • Additional Expenses: Parking fees, food during travel, any extra charges due to changes in the plan or route, local cab costs
  • Refunds: No refunds for missed sightseeing points due to unforeseen issues
