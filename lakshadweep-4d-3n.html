Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Agatti Island

Arrive at Agatti Island via Air India or IndiGo. Our representative will greet you at the airport and escort you to your hotel. Enjoy a welcome drink and lunch. After some relaxation, participate in a guided snorkeling session. End the day with a beachside evening and dinner at 8:30 PM. Overnight stay at Agatti.

Day 2: Day Trip to Thinnakara Island

After breakfast, embark on a private boat ride to the uninhabited Thinnakara Island. Enjoy snorkeling, swimming with turtles, and exploring a shipwreck. Visit the sandbanks near Bangaram Island before returning to Agatti. Dinner and overnight stay at Agatti.

Day 3: Water Sports and Kalpitti Island

Start your day with a glass-bottom boat ride. Additional water sports like kayaking, banana rides, and snorkeling can be arranged at an extra cost. After lunch, visit Kalpitti Island for turtle watching. Return to Agatti for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 4: Departure

Complete departure formalities and head to Agatti Airport for your return journey to Kochi.
