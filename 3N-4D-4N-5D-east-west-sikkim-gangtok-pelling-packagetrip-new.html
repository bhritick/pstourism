Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Gangtok Stay

  • Pick-up: From NJP Railway Station or Bagdogra Airport.
  • Drive: Through breathtaking landscapes to Gangtok.
  • Check-in: At a deluxe/2-star hotel and relax.
  • Evening: Free to explore MG Marg, the vibrant heart of Gangtok, perfect for shopping and local cuisine.
  • Overnight Stay: Gangtok.

Day 2: Gangtok Sightseeing – Changu Lake & Baba Mandir

  • Breakfast: Start your day with a delicious breakfast.
  • Visit: Iconic landmarks such as:
  • Changu Lake (Tsomgo Lake): A glacial lake at 12,400 ft, offering serene and snowy landscapes.
  • Baba Mandir: A shrine dedicated to Baba Harbhajan Singh, surrounded by captivating folklore.
  • Nathula Pass (Optional): Visit the historic Indo-China border (entry requires an additional permit).
  • Return: To your hotel for a relaxing evening.
  • Overnight Stay: Gangtok.

Day 3: Explore Pelling

  • Travel: To Pelling, known for its spectacular views of Mt. Kanchenjunga.
  • Explore: Iconic sights including:
  • Pelling Glass Skywalk: Asia's highest glass skywalk with stunning valley views.
  • Sangachoeling Monastery: A spiritual haven and one of Sikkim's oldest monasteries.
  • Check-in: At a deluxe hotel and enjoy the peaceful surroundings.
  • Overnight Stay: Pelling.

Day 4: Departure

  • After breakfast: Check out and transfer back to NJP Railway Station/Bagdogra Airport with beautiful memories.

Day 4: Pelling Sightseeing

  • Explore: More of Pelling's natural and cultural treasures:
  • Rimbi River & Rimbi Orange Garden: A picturesque spot ideal for nature lovers.
  • Khecheopalri Lake: A sacred lake known for its tranquility.
  • Kanchenjunga Waterfalls: A majestic waterfall cascading from great heights.
  • Darap Village: A quaint village showcasing Sikkimese culture.
  • Overnight Stay: Pelling.

Day 5: Departure

  • After breakfast: Transfer to NJP Railway Station/Bagdogra Airport.
