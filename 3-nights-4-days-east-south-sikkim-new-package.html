Day 1: Arrival in Gangtok

Pickup Point: NJP Railway Station/Bagdogra Airport (as per client preference).
Check-in to your Deluxe Hotel in Gangtok.
Spend the evening exploring the vibrant MG Marg or relaxing at the hotel.
Meals: Lunch & Dinner

Day 2: Gangtok Sightseeing

Visit:
Tsomgo Lake - A mesmerizing glacial lake surrounded by snow-clad mountains.
Ropeway Ride - Experience breathtaking panoramic views (optional).
Baba Mandir - A sacred shrine amidst stunning landscapes.
Nathula Pass (optional, at an additional cost) - Visit the Indo-China border with breathtaking views.
Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Day 3: Gangtok to Ravangla

Morning transfer to Ravangla (approx. 2.5 hours).
Explore:
Temi Tea Garden - Witness the lush greenery and learn about tea cultivation.
Buddha Park - A serene and spiritual destination with stunning Buddha statues.
Namchi Chardham (optional, at an additional cost) - A cultural and spiritual hub showcasing replicas of India's famous temples.
Check-in to your hotel in Ravangla for an overnight stay.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Day 4: Departure from Ravangla

Check out from the hotel.
Transfer to NJP Railway Station/Bagdogra Airport for departure.
End of the trip with wonderful memories of Sikkim.
Meals: Breakfast
