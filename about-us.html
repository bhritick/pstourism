Why Choose Pather Sandhane TourisM (PST)?

At Pather Sandhane TourisM (PST), we are dedicated to delivering exceptional travel experiences with a strong focus on quality, affordability, and safety. As an ISO 9001:2015 certified travel agency, we provide a wide range of services under PST Holidayz, PST Hotel and Homestay, and PSCab.

Services We Offer

  • Holiday Packages under PST Holidayz
  • Hotel Bookings with PST Hotel and Homestay
  • Cab Services through PSCab
  • Bus and Bike Rentals
  • Pre-wedding and Honeymoon Packages
  • Group Tours, Educational Trips, and Business Travel Solutions

Meet the Founder: Sayan Chatterjee

Sayan Chatterjee, Founder and CEO of PST Holidayz, holds a B.Sc. in Physics and an M.Sc. in Biomedical Instrumentation from the University of Calcutta. After working as a teacher in various ICSE schools across India, his passion for travel led him to start vlogging on YouTube. Driven by the desire to provide affordable, quality travel services, he founded Pather Sandhane TourisM (PST), which has since served over 500 clients from India and Bangladesh. Currently, he also works as an Assistant Branch Post Master with India Post. In addition to his professional pursuits, Sayan has been actively involved in social work, especially during the COVID-19 pandemic and the aftermath of Cyclone Yash in the Sundarbans.
