Pickup & Drop

  • Pickup & Drop - NJP / SLG / IXB
  • Tour Duration: 4 Nights / 5 Days

Day 1:

NJP to Sillerygaon

  • Pickup : From NJP, Siliguri, or Bagdogra.
  • Drive : Enjoy scenic routes via Teesta Bridge and Kalijhora.
  • Arrival : Reach the charming village of Sillerygaon, nestled in pine forests.
  • Check-In : Settle into your homestay.

Day 2:

Sillerygaon to Zuluk

  • After Breakfast : Travel to Zuluk, passing Rongpo River, Aritar Lake, and Siddhivinayak Mandir.
  • Scenic Highlights : Admire the stunning Zigzag Road and Quekhola Falls.
  • Check-In : Arrive and check in at your homestay in Zuluk or Lungthung
  • Overnight Stay : Enjoy the night in Zuluk or Lungthung.

Day 3:

Zuluk to PadamChen

  • Depart from Zulukt : Head towards PadamChen.
  • En Route :Visit Thambi View Point, Laxmi Chowk, Nathang Valley, and Elephant Lake/Kupup Lake.
  • Explore :See Tukla Pass and the historic Old Baba Mandir.
  • Arrive and Check-In : Reach PadamChen and settle into your homestay.
  • Overnight Stay : Enjoy the night in PadamChen or Lingtam.

Day 4:

PadamChen to Rishikhola/Rorethang/Rolep

  • Travel : Depart for Rishikhola or Rorethang.
  • Relax : Enjoy the serene Reshi River and explore the natural beauty.
  • Check-In : Arrive and check in at your homestay.
  • Overnight Stay : Stay overnight in Rishikhola, Rorethang, or Rolep.

Day 5:

Rishikhola/Rorethang to NJP

  • After Breakfast : Start your return journey to NJP.
  • Drop-Off : You'll be dropped at NJP, Siliguri, or Bagdogra.
  • Tour Ends : Conclude your trip with cherished memories.
