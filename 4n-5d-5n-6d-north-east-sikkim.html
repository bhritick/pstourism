Unforgettable North Sikkim Exploration

Join us for a 5 Days 4 Nights or 6 Days 5 Nights tour package, exploring the stunning landscapes of North Sikkim with Pather Sandhane Tourism.

Book Now

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Transfer to Gangtok

Pick up from NJP station/Bagdogra Airport and transfer to Gangtok for an overnight stay.

Day 2: North Sikkim Exploration - Lachen

Begin your journey to Lachen, with sightseeing stops along the way. Overnight stay in Lachen.

Day 3: Gurudongmar Lake & Transfer to Lachung

Visit the serene Gurudongmar Lake in the morning and later transfer to Lachung for an overnight stay.

Day 4: Zero Point and Return to Gangtok

Explore Zero Point and enjoy some local sightseeing before heading back to Gangtok. Overnight stay in Gangtok.

Day 5: Drop at NJP Station/Bagdogra Airport

For the 5 Days 4 Nights package, this is the final day. You will be dropped off at NJP Station or Bagdogra Airport for your return journey.

Day 6 (For 6 Days 5 Nights Package): Visit Tsomgo Lake, Baba Mandir & Nathula Pass

On the 6th day, visit Tsomgo Lake, Baba Mandir, and Nathula Pass before being dropped off at the station or airport.
