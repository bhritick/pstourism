3 Nights 4 Days North Bengal Tour

Explore Sittong, Darjeeling, and Mirik with Pather Sandhane Tourism (ISO 9001:2015 certified).

Book Now

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Transfer to Sittong

Pick-up from NJP station, transfer to Sittong with sightseeing at Ahaldhara View Point, Namthing Pokhri, and Mahaldhiram Tea Garden. Overnight stay in Sittong.

Day 2: Sittong to Darjeeling

Visit Yogighat Bridge, Rabindra Museum, Lamahata Eco Park, Takdah Tinchule View Point, and Pine Forest. Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 3: Darjeeling Sightseeing

Full day sightseeing including Batasia Loop, Ghoom Monastery, Peace Pagoda, Japanese Temple, Tenzing Rock, and Darjeeling Zoo. Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 4: Departure via NJP with Sightseeing

Visit Mirik Lake, Pashupati Market, Simana View Point, and Bokar Monastery. Drop at NJP station for your return journey.

Package Inclusions & Exclusions

Inclusions:

  • All meals from Day 1 lunch to breakfast on the checkout day.
  • Standard/Deluxe/Premium lodging as per the package.
  • 4-seater personal cab for all transfers and sightseeing.
  • Driver allowance and fuel costs.

Exclusions:

  • Entry fees, tickets, and parking charges.
  • Meals during the journey and personal expenses.
  • Any changes in the itinerary or route diversion charges.
