Day-Wise Itinerary for Gangtok Tour

Day 1: Arrival & Transfer to Gangtok

  • Arrival at NJP Railway Station or Bagdogra Airport.
  • Transfer to Gangtok (approx. 4-5 hours drive).
  • Check-in at your Gangtok hotel and relax.
  • Evening free to explore MG Marg, Gangtok's vibrant shopping and dining hub.
  • Overnight Stay: Gangtok.

Day 2: Gangtok - Lachen

  • After breakfast, start your journey to Lachen (approx. 6-7 hours drive).
  • En route, enjoy the scenic beauty of Seven Sisters Waterfalls, Naga Waterfalls, and Chungthang Valley.
  • Arrive at Lachen and check-in at your accommodation.
  • Overnight Stay: Lachen.

Day 3: Lachen - Gurudongmar Lake - Lachung

  • After an early breakfast, drive to Gurudongmar Lake (17,800 ft), one of the highest lakes in the world.
  • Enjoy the spiritual atmosphere and stunning views of snow-capped peaks.
  • Return to Lachen for lunch, then proceed to Lachung (approx. 2-3 hours drive).
  • Check-in at your Lachung accommodation.
  • Overnight Stay: Lachung.

Day 4: Lachung - Yumthang Valley - Lachung

  • After breakfast, head to Yumthang Valley (12,000 ft), also known as the Valley of Flowers, offering breathtaking views of colorful blooms and snow-covered mountains.
  • Visit the Hot Springs in the valley, known for their therapeutic properties.
  • Return to Lachung for lunch and relax.
  • Overnight Stay: Lachung.

Day 5: Lachung - Drive Back to Gangtok

  • After breakfast, drive back to Gangtok (approx. 6-7 hours drive), enjoying the scenic landscapes along the way.
  • Check-in at your Gangtok hotel and relax.
  • Overnight Stay: Gangtok.

Day 6: Departure

  • After breakfast, check out and transfer to NJP Railway Station or Bagdogra Airport.
  • Depart with unforgettable memories of North Sikkim’s beauty.
