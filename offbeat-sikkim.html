9 Nights, 9 Days Special North Sikkim Trip

Explore the majestic North Sikkim with Pather Sandhane Tourism, an ISO 9001:2015 certified travel organization

Day 1 - Pickup from NJP & Transfer to Gangtok

Arrival at NJP station and transfer to Gangtok.

Overnight stay in Gangtok.

Day 2 - Journey to Dzongu

After breakfast, start your journey to Dzongu, a pristine and tranquil area in North Sikkim known for its lush landscapes, waterfalls, and views of Mount Kanchenjunga. Dzongu is home to the indigenous Lepcha community, offering a glimpse of their rich culture and traditional way of life.

Overnight stay in Dzongu.

Day 3 - Dzongu Sightseeing

Explore the charming villages of Thingvong and Lingthem in Dzongu. Both offer breathtaking views of the Kanchenjunga range, rich biodiversity, and authentic Lepcha cultural experiences.

  • Thingvong Village
  • Lingthem Village

Overnight stay in Dzongu.

Day 4 - Transfer to Lachen

After breakfast, transfer to Lachen with sightseeing stops along the way.

Overnight stay in Lachen.

Day 5 - Lachen Sightseeing & Transfer to Lachung

Visit the famous Gurudongmar Lake, one of the highest lakes in the world. Afterward, transfer to Lachung.

Overnight stay in Lachung.

Day 6 - Lachung Sightseeing

  • Yumthang Valley
  • Zero Point

Overnight stay in Lachung.

Day 7 - Sightseeing of Mount Katao & Songlaphu Lake

Explore Mount Katao, Songlaphu Lake, and relax in a natural hot spring.

Overnight stay in Lachung.

Day 8 - Return to Gangtok

After breakfast, return back to Gangtok.

Overnight stay in Gangtok.

Day 9 - Departure

After breakfast, drop-off at NJP Station for your return journey.

Inclusions

  • - All quality meals (from Day 1 Lunch to Day 9 Breakfast)
  • - Lodging for 8 nights in deluxe accommodations
  • - Sightseeing as per itinerary
  • - Pick-up, drop-off, and transfers
  • - Permits, driver allowance, and travel meals

Exclusions

  • - Entry fees, tickets, parking charges
  • - Zero Point & Songlaphu Lake extra costs
  • - Any sudden route changes due to natural calamities or unavoidable circumstances
