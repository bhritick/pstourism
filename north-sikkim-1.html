Why Choose a North Sikkim Tour with PS Tourism?

North Sikkim is a hidden gem in Northeast India, offering a unique blend of natural beauty and cultural richness. Here’s why it should be on your travel bucket list:

Untouched Natural Beauty

From snow-capped peaks to crystal-clear lakes, North Sikkim is a visual treat.

Adventure Opportunities

Trekking, river rafting, and yak rides await thrill-seekers.

Spiritual Vibes

Visit ancient monasteries like Phodong and Lachung to experience peace and tranquility.

Top Attractions to Explore

Gurudongmar Lake

One of the highest lakes in the world, its turquoise waters and snow-clad mountains create a surreal landscape.

Yumthang Valley

Known as the "Valley of Flowers," it’s a colorful paradise filled with rhododendrons and hot springs.

Zero Point

Located at 15,300 feet, it offers panoramic views of the Himalayas.

Best Time to Plan Your Trip

The ideal time for a North Sikkim Tour is between March and May or October and December. Winter is perfect for snow lovers, while spring brings blooming flowers in Yumthang Valley.
