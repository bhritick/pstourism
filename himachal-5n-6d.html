Quotation

5 Nights 6 Days Holiday Trip

  • Shimla: 2 Nights
  • Manali: 3 Nights

Starting from Chandigarh, ending in Chandigarh.

Hotel Used: Budget Category / Premium depending on package cost.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Shimla (120 kms, 4 hrs)

Pickup from Chandigarh & drop-off at the hotel in Shimla. Popular hotspots en route include Pinjaur Garden, Barog, and Solan. Evening stay and dinner at the prescribed hotel in Shimla.

Day 2: Trip to Kufri (13 km) - Night stay at Shimla

After breakfast, head for sightseeing in Shimla and Kufri. Kufri is famous for scenic beauty and sports activities at 2,622 meters altitude. Enjoy yak and horse rides, and trek to the Mahasu Peak. Evening free for shopping at “The Mall & The Ridge.” Overnight stay at Shimla hotel.

Day 3: Shimla to Manali

After breakfast, checkout and proceed towards Manali with stops at Kullu river rafting point, Kullu shawl factory, Dusseshra ground, Naggar Valley, Roerich Art Gallery, and Manikaran. Check-in to the hotel at Manali and enjoy the beauty of this hill station. Overnight stay at Manali.

Day 4: Manali Sightseeing

Visit Mall Road, Tibetan Monastery, Vashist Village, Hadimba Devi Temple, Manu Temple. Evening free for leisure and overnight stay at Manali.

Day 5: Excursion to Solang Valley

Visit Solang Valley for adventure activities like parachuting, paragliding, zorbing. Explore the lush greenery and apple orchards. Return to Manali and enjoy the evening at your own leisure. Overnight stay at Manali.

Day 6: Departure to Chandigarh

After checkout, return to Chandigarh with sweet memories of your trip.
