Trip to Nilgiri's Jungle-Ooty-Mysore

Join us for an unforgettable 5-night, 6-day adventure through the stunning landscapes of Mysore, Madumalai, and Ooty with Pather Sandhane Tourism, powered by IQ PLUS VLOGS. Experience the blend of rich cultural heritage, vibrant wildlife, and the scenic beauty of South India.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Mysore/Bangalore

After your arrival at Mysore or Bangalore, you'll be transferred to your hotel for check-in. The rest of the day is free for you to relax and explore the city at your convenience.

Staying at: Mysore

Day 2: Mysore Sightseeing

Mysore, known for its palaces, vibrant festivals, and rich cultural history, offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity. After breakfast, explore the following attractions:

  • Jaganmohan Palace (Art Gallery)
  • Mysore Zoo
  • Chamundi Temple
  • Mahishasura Statue
  • Big Bull (Nandi)
  • Mysore Palace
  • St. Philomena's Church

Staying at: Mysore

Day 3: Jungle Safari & Transfer to Ooty

After breakfast, check out and start your journey to the Nilgiris. On the way, embark on a thrilling jungle safari in the Madumalai and Bandipur National Parks. Post-safari, proceed to Ooty for check-in and rest.

  • Madumalai National Park
  • Bandipur National Park Safari
  • Needle Rock View Point
  • Pykara Dam

Staying at: Ooty

Day 4: Ooty Sightseeing

After breakfast, explore the lush landscapes and scenic spots of Ooty:

  • Ooty Rose Garden
  • 9th Mile Shooting Point
  • Ooty Botanical Garden
  • Doddabetta Peak
  • Ooty Tea Factory

Staying at: Ooty

Day 5: More Ooty Adventures

Continue exploring Ooty's natural beauty, including:

  • Kamraj Sagar Dam
  • 6th Mile
  • Pine Tree Forest
  • Wenlock Downs

Staying at: Ooty

Day 6: Departure

After breakfast, check out and head back to Mysore or Bangalore for your departure.

Key Attractions

Doddabetta Peak

Doddabetta Peak, standing at 2,637 meters, is the highest mountain in the Nilgiri Hills. It offers panoramic views of the surrounding landscape, making it a must-visit for nature lovers.

Madumalai Jungle Safari

Embark on an exciting jungle safari through Madumalai Wildlife Sanctuary, home to a wide variety of animals like tigers, elephants, leopards, and diverse bird species. Experience the natural beauty of the region.

Pykara Dam

Pykara Dam, built on the Pykara River, is a serene tourist spot. Visitors can enjoy boating in the reservoir, explore the nearby Pykara Falls, and enjoy the lush greenery surrounding the area.

Bandipur National Park Safari

Another thrilling safari experience awaits you at Bandipur National Park, where you can spot wildlife in their natural habitat, including elephants, deer, and possibly even tigers!
