5 Nights 6 Days Darjeeling Honeymoon Package

Experience a romantic getaway with Pather Sandhane Tourism.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Darjeeling

Pickup from NJP Station/Bagdogra Airport and transfer to Darjeeling. Spend the evening at Darjeeling Mall and explore the local market. Enjoy a complimentary honeymoon welcome drink.

Day 2: Darjeeling Sightseeing

Visit Tiger Hill for a sunrise view of Kanchenjunga, followed by visits to Ghoom Monastery, Peace Pagoda, Tenzing Rock, and more.

Day 3 & 4: Leisure Days or Offbeat Exploration

Customize your plans. You can either relax or explore offbeat destinations around Darjeeling, tailored to your preferences.

Day 5: Transfer to Mirik

Transfer to Mirik with visits to Gopaldhara Tea Estate, Mirik Lake, Pashupati Market, and more. Relax by the Rangbhang River in the evening.

Day 6: Departure

After breakfast, transfer to NJP Station or Bagdogra Airport for your departure.
