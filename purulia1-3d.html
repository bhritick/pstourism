Purulia Trail

Explore the scenic beauty of Purulia with Pather Sandhane Tourism (ISO 9001:2015 certified).

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Exploration

Pick-up from Borabhum Station. Visit Pardi Dam, Pakhi Pahar, and Matha Pahar. Stay overnight at a homestay in Ayodhya, Purulia.

Day 2: Scenic Sightseeing

Explore Loharia Mandir, Kestobazar Dam, Sita Kunda, Durga Mandir, Ram Mandir, and Mayur Pahar. Visit Murguma Dam, Marble Lake, Bamni Falls, Thurga Falls, and Thurga Dam. Return to the Ajodhya foothill homestay.

Day 3: The Grand Finale

Visit Joychandi Pahar, Baranti Lake, Panchet Dam, and Garhpanchakot. Drop-off at Purulia Station.
