5 Night 6 Days Trip to Nilgiri's Jungle, Ooty, & Mysore

Book Now

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Mysore/Bangalore

Pick up from Mysore/Bangalore and transfer to the hotel. Enjoy the rest of the day at your convenience. Overnight stay at Mysore.

Day 2: Explore Mysore

Mysore is famous for its palaces, vibrant festivals, and rich cultural heritage. Visit:

  • Jaganmohan Palace (Art Gallery)
  • Mysore Zoo & Shopping
  • Chamundi Temple & Statue of Mahishasura
  • Big Bull (Nandi)
  • Mysore Maharaja Palace
  • St. Philomena's Church

Overnight stay at Mysore.

Day 3: Mysore to Ooty via Madumalai Jungle Safari

After breakfast, check out early and start the journey to the Nilgiris. Enjoy a safari at Madumalai Tiger Reserve and Bandipur Forest. Transfer to Ooty and visit:

  • Needle Rock View Point
  • Pine Forest
  • Pykara Dam

Overnight stay at Ooty.

Day 4: Ooty Sightseeing

  • Ooty Rose Garden
  • 9th Mile Shooting Point
  • Ooty Botanical Garden
  • Doddabetta Peak
  • Ooty Tea Factory

Overnight stay at Ooty.

Day 5: Ooty Sightseeing

  • Kamraj Sagar Dam
  • 6th Mile, Mund
  • Pine Tree Forest
  • Wenlock Downs

Overnight stay at Ooty.

Day 6: Departure

Drop at Mysore/Bangalore after breakfast. End of tour.

Key Sightseeing Highlights

Doddabetta Peak

The highest mountain in the Nilgiri Hills, offering panoramic views of the surrounding landscapes.

Madumalai Jungle Safari

Explore Madumalai Wildlife Sanctuary with trained experts and witness a rich diversity of flora and fauna, including elephants and tigers.

Pykara Dam

A scenic dam near Ooty, known for its tranquil surroundings and boating opportunities.
