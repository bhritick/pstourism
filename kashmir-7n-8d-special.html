Jannat-E-Kashmir Tour Package

Duration: 7 Nights / 8 Days

Certified: PSTourism - An ISO 9001:2015 Certified Travel Company

Website: www.pstourism.in

Itinerary Highlights

Day 1: Arrival - Jammu to Katra

  • Arrival in Jammu and transfer to Katra.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel in Katra.

Day 2: Katra – Mata Vaishno Devi Darshan

  • Post-breakfast trek for the spiritual darshan of Mata Vaishno Devi.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 3: Katra to Pahalgam (255 km | 8 hrs)

  • Journey to Pahalgam, “The Valley of Shepherds.”
  • Enroute stops at Jawaharlal Tunnel, saffron fields, and Awantipora ruins.
  • Enjoy scenic views and bridle walks.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel in Pahalgam.

Day 4: Pahalgam to Srinagar

  • Morning drive to Srinagar.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel in Srinagar.

Day 5: Srinagar - Sonamarg - Srinagar (166 km round trip)

  • Full-day excursion to Sonamarg, “The Meadow of Gold.”
  • Optional activities: Horse riding to Thajwas Glacier and sledging.
  • Return to Srinagar for an overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 6: Srinagar to Gulmarg (52 km)

  • Drive to Gulmarg, famous for its ski slopes and golf course.
  • Optional Gondola ride to Phase 1 and Phase 2.
  • Return to Srinagar for an overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 7: Srinagar to Doodhpathri - Srinagar (100 km round trip)

  • Visit Doodhpathri, the serene “Valley of Milk.”
  • Complimentary one-hour Shikara ride on Dal Lake.
  • Overnight stay in a deluxe houseboat.

Day 8: Srinagar - Departure

  • Transfer to Srinagar Airport for onward journey.

Inclusions

  • Accommodation in 2-star or 3-star hotels (as per the package).
  • Meal Plan: Breakfast and dinner (MAPI).
  • Transportation: Tempo Traveller for the entire tour.
  • Complimentary one-hour Shikara ride on Dal Lake.

Exclusions

  • Airfare or train fare.
  • Meals not mentioned in the itinerary.
  • Personal expenses (tips, laundry, phone calls).
  • Entry fees to sightseeing locations (e.g., Mughal Gardens ₹50-100).
  • Optional activities: Gondola rides, pony rides, sledging, rafting.
  • Local cabs for Aru Valley, Betaab Valley, and Chandanwari (₹1,800-2,000 per vehicle).
  • Snow vehicle charges from Tangmarg to Gulmarg.

Additional Highlights

  • Prepaid SIM cards do not work in Jammu & Kashmir; only postpaid connections are allowed.
  • Local taxis are mandatory for select areas, as per local union rules.
  • Customizable tour options available.
