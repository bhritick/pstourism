Day 1: Arrival and Exploration

We will board a boat from Gadkhali and head towards Pakhirala, witnessing the unique beauty of nature along the way. The boat will be equipped with all facilities. When the boat approaches the bird-filled jungle, your heart will rejoice. Thousands of birds will welcome you during this time. Before the evening sets in, we will return to the hotel/cottage where a delicious tiffin will be waiting. Our cottage is a beautiful one situated in the heart of the Sundarbans, surrounded by rivers. You can take a serene evening walk along the river, but remember not to venture into the water. A delightful dinner will be served at night

Day 2: Jungle Safari and River Exploration

Early in the morning, we will go to the boat with sleepy eyes to witness a beautiful sunrise over the river. The reflection of the sky on the water and the emergence of the sun will leave your heart filled with joy. We will have breakfast on the boat itself and then explore places like Sajnekhali, Sudhanyakhali, Banbibi, Bhuribunia, Gazikhali, Pirakhali, and Panchmukhani. If luck favors, you might catch glimpses of the Royal Bengal Tiger, deer, wild boar, jungle fowl, and various other wildlife of Sundarbans. I Night stay at Cottage/Resort
