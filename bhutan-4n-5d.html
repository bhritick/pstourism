Bhutan Tour Package - 4N 5D

Discover the magic of Bhutan with our exclusive 4-night, 5-day tour package. Immerse yourself in the pristine beauty, rich culture, and spiritual serenity of Bhutan with Pather Sandhane Tourism, powered by IQ Plus Vlogs.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Jaigaon - Phuentsholing to Thimphu

Arrive at Jaigaon/Phuentsholing and begin your journey to Thimphu. Enjoy some sightseeing along the way before reaching Thimphu for your night stay.

Staying at: Thimphu

Day 2: Thimphu to Punakha

After breakfast, drive to Punakha, crossing the scenic Dochu-La Pass. Visit the majestic Punakha Dzong, the Suspension Bridge, and enjoy an adventurous river rafting experience. Return to Thimphu for a night stay.

Staying at: Thimphu

Day 3: Thimphu Sightseeing & Transfer to Paro

Thimphu, the capital of Bhutan, is known for its rich cultural and royal heritage. After breakfast, explore the city’s landmarks including:

  • Buddha Point (a giant Buddha statue overlooking the valley)
  • BBS Tower
  • Takin National Zoo (home to Bhutan’s national animal, the Takin)
  • Simply Bhutan Museum
  • Clock Tower Square
  • Kaja Throm

In the evening, transfer to Paro for an overnight stay.

Staying at: Paro

Day 4: Paro Local Sightseeing

Explore the scenic and cultural highlights of Paro, starting with a visit to Paro town and its famous international airport viewpoint. Other attractions include:

  • Tiger’s Nest Monastery (Trekking)
  • Nya Mey Zam Bridge
  • Zinpung Dzong
  • Kichu Lakhang (one of the oldest temples in Bhutan)

Staying at: Paro

Day 5: Paro to Phuentsholing - Jaigaon Drop

After breakfast, drive back to Phuentsholing/Jaigaon where your tour ends with wonderful memories of Bhutan.
