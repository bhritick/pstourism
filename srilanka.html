Day 3: Kandy to Nuwara Eliya

  • After breakfast, embark on a scenic journey to Nuwara Eliya.
  • Enrich your experience with a city tour of Kandy, featuring the Arts and Crafts Center, Gem Museum, and picturesque Kandy Lake.
  • Visit a Tea Plantation and Factory, explore the Hanuman Temple and Ramboda Waterfalls.
  • Upon arrival in Nuwara Eliya, check in at your hotel, then visit Ashok Vatika and Sita Devi Temple.
  • Overnight stay in Nuwara Eliya.

Day 4: Nuwara Eliya to Mirissa via Ella

  • After checkout, travel to Mirissa with a stopover in Ella.

  • Check in at your hotel in Mirissa and unwind.
