Tour Itinerary

Day 01: Deoghar

Pickup from Jasidih station and transfer to Deoghar (Baba Baidyanath Dham). Participate in Puja with a local panda. Afternoon sightseeing includes:

  • Trikut Hill
  • Satsang Asram
  • Nolokha Mandir
  • Nandan Pahar

Explore local food, stay at Deoghar.

Day 02: Deoghar to Rajgir

Take a train from Deoghar to Baktiyarpur by Patna Memu Express Special (6:50 AM to 11:50 AM) or Cab.

Transfer to Rajgir with sightseeing of Pavapuri, Nalanda, Kundalpur. Stay at Rajgir.

Day 03: Rajgir Local Sightseeing

Visit popular attractions in Rajgir:

  • Pandu Pokhar
  • Viswa Shanti Stupa
  • Venuvana
  • Japanese Stupa
  • Vulture's Peak or Griddhakuta
  • Makhdum Kund
  • Bimbisara's Jail

Stay overnight at Rajgir.

Day 04: Bodh Gaya & Gaya Sightseeing

Visit the famous attractions in Bodh Gaya and Gaya, including:

  • Mahabodhi Temple
  • Bodhi Tree
  • Great Buddha Statue
  • Thai Monastery
  • Cankamana
  • Muchakinda Lake

Drop at Gaya Station after sightseeing.

Day 05: Departure

After breakfast, transfer to NJP Station or Airport with sweet memories of the trip.
