Trip Itinerary

Day 1: Pickup from Suvarnabhumi Airport

Upon arrival at Suvarnabhumi Airport, enjoy a comfortable transfer to Wellness Stay & Hotel Sukhumvit 107, Bangkok. Relax and unwind for the night at your hotel.

Day 2: Safari World and Marine Park with Lunch

Join us for an exhilarating expedition to Safari World and Marine Park, inclusive of a delightful lunch (closed on Mondays). Encounter diverse wildlife and witness captivating marine spectacles. Overnight stay at Bangkok hotel.

Day 3: Transfer to Mike Beach Resort, Pattaya

Transfer from Wellness Stay & Hotel Sukhumvit 107, Bangkok to Mike Beach Resort, Pattaya. Overnight stay in Pattaya.

Day 4: Coral Island Tour with Lunch by Speed Boat

Embark on a thrilling Coral Island Tour by speedboat from Pattaya. Enjoy pristine beaches, vibrant coral reefs, and a delicious lunch amidst stunning tropical surroundings. Overnight stay in Pattaya.

Day 5: Departure

Your trip comes to an end. Transfer back to Suvarnabhumi Airport, Bangkok for your return journey.

Note: Please stay in the hotel lobby at the designated time for transfers. The driver will not wait more than 5 minutes.
