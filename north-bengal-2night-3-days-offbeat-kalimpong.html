Kolakham Holiday Package - 2 Nights, 3 Days Tour

Experience serenity in North Bengal with Pather Sandhane Tourism

Day 1 - Arrival at New Mal/New Jalpaiguri Junction & Transfer to Kolakham

Begin your scenic journey to Kolakham via:

  • Gorubathan
  • Chel River
  • Ambiok Tea Garden
  • Buddha Statue at Paparkheti
  • Neora Valley Jungle

Overnight stay in Kolakham.

Day 2 - Kolakham Sightseeing

After breakfast, explore the stunning landscapes of Kolakham:

  • Changey Waterfalls
  • Lava Monastery
  • Lava Bazaar
  • I Love Lava View Point
  • Rishop View Point

Overnight stay in Kolakham.

Day 3 - Departure & Enroute Sightseeing

After breakfast, proceed to New Mal/New Jalpaiguri Junction. Enroute visit:

  • Damdim Monastery

Transfer to the railway station for your onward journey.
