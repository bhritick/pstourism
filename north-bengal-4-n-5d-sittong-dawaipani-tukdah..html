4 Nights 5 Days Holiday Package

Explore the enchanting landscapes of North Bengal: Sittong, Dawaipani, Takdah, Darjeeling, and Mirik with Pather Sandhane Tourism (ISO 9001:2015 certified).

Book Now

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Sittong from NJP

Pick-up from NJP station and transfer to Sittong. Enjoy sightseeing at:

  • Namching Pokri
  • Mahaldhiram Tea Garden
  • Ahaldhara Viewpoint
  • Yogighat Bridge
  • Rabindra Museum

Accommodation: Overnight stay at Sittong.

Day 2: Sittong to Dawaipani

Travel to Dawaipani with stops at:

  • Lover's Meet View Point
  • Pesak Tea Garden

Accommodation: Overnight stay at Dawaipani.

Day 3: Dawaipani to Takdah

Enjoy scenic views at:

  • Gumbadhara View Point
  • Takdah Tinchule View Point

Accommodation: Overnight stay at Takdah.

Day 4: Takdah to Darjeeling

Travel to Darjeeling via Lamahata Eco Park. Enjoy a lively evening at the Mall.

Accommodation: Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Optional Activities: Add an extra day for sightseeing in Darjeeling, visiting places like Batasia Loop, Ghoom Monastery, Peace Pagoda, and more.

Day 5: Departure from NJP/IXB/SLG

Before departure, enjoy sightseeing at:

  • Mirik Lake
  • Pasupati Market
  • Simana Viewpoint
  • Bokar Monastery
  • Gopal Dhara Tea Garden

Transfer back to NJP/IXB/SLG for your onward journey.

Package Inclusions

  • All quality meals from Day 1 lunch to check-out day breakfast
  • Comfortable lodging throughout the trip
  • Cab for sightseeing, pick-up, and drop transfers
  • Driver allowance and related expenses
