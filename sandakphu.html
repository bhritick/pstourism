Trip Itinerary

Day 1: NJP to Tunglu

  • Arrival at NJP Station and proceed towards Manebhanjan via:
    • Tingling View Point
    • Mirik Lake
    • Pasupati Phatak (optional, extra cost)
    • Gopaldhara Tea Estate
    • Nepal Simana View Point
  • Permission at Manebhanjan and transfer to Tunglu by Landrover, passing:
    • Chitray Monastery
    • Meghma
    • Tonglu
  • Overnight stay at Tunglu in a hotel/homestay.

Day 2: Tunglu to Sandakphu

  • Early morning visit to Tumling Sunrise Point.
  • After breakfast, proceed to Sandakphu via:
    • Gairibans
    • Singalila National Park
    • Kalapokhari
    • Sandakphu View Point
  • Overnight stay at Sandakphu in a hotel/homestay.

Day 3: Sandakphu to Majidhura/Dhotray

  • Witness a stunning sunrise at Sandakphu View Point.
  • Depart Sandakphu for Manebhanjan and transfer to Majidhura/Dhotray.
  • Overnight stay at Majidhura/Dhotray in a hotel/homestay.
