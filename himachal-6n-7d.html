Explore the stunning beauty of Himachal Pradesh with this exclusive 7-day tour, offering a blend of spiritual serenity, scenic vistas, and thrilling adventures. From Shimla’s charming Mall Road to the mystical valleys of Kasol and Manali, this tour is designed to provide a wholesome experience.

Tour Itinerary – 6 Nights & 7 Days

Day 1: Arrival in Chandigarh/Shimla

Pickup from Chandigarh or Shimla Railway Station.

Transfer to Shimla for a comfortable stay.

Evening leisure at Shimla Mall Road (walking distance).

Overnight Stay: Shimla

Day 2: Shimla Local Sightseeing

  • Taradevi Temple (entry charges extra)
  • Kufri for adventure sports and panoramic views
  • Green Valley – a scenic meadow surrounded by dense forest
  • Sonkot Mochon Temple
  • Jakoo Temple, famous for its large Hanuman statue
  • Evening stroll around Shimla Mall Road (walking distance)

Overnight Stay: Shimla

Day 3: Journey to Manikaran/Kasol

Depart for Manikaran or Kasol, enroute visit:

  • Sundernagar Lake – a beautiful man-made reservoir
  • Pandoh Dam, a hydroelectric powerhouse with scenic views

Overnight Stay: Manikaran/Kasol (depending on the package category)

Day 4: Manikaran/Kasol to Manali

  • Manikaran Gurudwara – known for its hot springs
  • Explore Kasol – the hippie paradise
  • Shopping in Kullu
  • River Rafting and Paragliding in Kullu

Overnight Stay: Manali

Day 5: Manali Local Sightseeing

  • Vasisth Temple (famous for its hot water springs)
  • Hidimba Devi Temple, an ancient cave temple
  • Club House for fun activities
  • Manu Temple (scenic walk)
  • Van Vihar – a nature lover’s paradise
  • Tibetan Monastery

Overnight Stay: Manali

Day 6: Excursion to Solang Valley & Rohtang Pass

Enjoy a thrilling day with sightseeing at:

  • Atal Tunnel & Rohtang Pass (if accessible, additional cost)
  • Solang Valley for adventure sports like paragliding, skiing, and more

Overnight Stay: Manali

Day 7: Departure to Chandigarh

After breakfast, drive back to Chandigarh Railway Station for drop-off.
