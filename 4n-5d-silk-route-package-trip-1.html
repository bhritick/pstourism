Tour Itinerary

Experience top-notch service with Pather Sandhane Tourism during this exciting Silk Route journey.

Day 1: Arrival and Transfer to Sillerygaon

Pickup from NJP Railway Station or IXB Airport and transfer to Sillerygaon. Free day to explore the beautiful, offbeat town of Sillerygaon.

Overnight Stay: Sillerygaon

Day 2: Sillerygaon to Rorethang / Rishikhola / Lingtam (Optional)

Travel from Sillerygaon to Rorethang, Rishikhola, or Lingtam (optional) with sightseeing along the way.

Day 3: Transfer to Zuluk

Journey from Rishikhola / Rorethang / Lingtam to Zuluk with sightseeing stops at Kue Khola Waterfalls, Aritar Lake, and the famous Zigzag Road.

Overnight Stay: Zuluk

Day 4: Zuluk to Gangtok with Sightseeing

Transfer to Gangtok with en-route sightseeing of Nathang Valley, Elephant Lake, Changu Lake, Baba Mandir, and Nathula Pass.

Overnight Stay: Gangtok

Day 5: Departure

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfer to NJP Railway Station or IXB Airport for your return journey.
