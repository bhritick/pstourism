4 Nights 5 Days Silk Route Trip

Experience the breathtaking Silk Route with Pather Sandhane Tourism (ISO 9001:2015 certified).

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Sillerygaon

Pickup from NJP station or IXB airport. Transfer to Sillerygaon for an overnight stay.

Day 2: Sillerygaon to Rorethang/Rishikhola/Lingtam

Scenic drive to your chosen destination with sightseeing along the way. Overnight stay in Rorethang/Rishikhola/Lingtam.

Day 3: Journey to Zuluk

Transfer to Zuluk, enjoying stops at Kue Khola Waterfalls and Aritar Lake. Overnight stay in Zuluk.

Day 4: Zuluk to Gangtok

Transfer to Gangtok with sightseeing at Nathang Valley, Changu Lake, and Baba Mandir. Overnight stay in Gangtok.

Day 5: Departure

Check-out and transfer back to NJP station to conclude your memorable journey.

Package Inclusions & Exclusions

Inclusions:

  • Meals from Day 1 lunch to breakfast on check-out day
  • Comfortable lodging throughout the trip
  • Personal cab for transfers and sightseeing
  • All necessary permits

Exclusions:

  • Entry fees, tickets, and parking charges
  • Food during travel times
  • Local transportation costs not included in the package
  • Extra costs due to route diversions

Important Information

Minimum of 2 participants required for booking. Options for Share Trip, Group Trip, or Personal Trip available.
