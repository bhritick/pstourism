Offbeat Tour with Kalimpong - 4 Nights, 5 Days

Get ready to experience the hidden gems of North Bengal with this 4-night, 5-day tour package! This offbeat trail will take you through some of the most scenic locations in Kalimpong and its surrounding areas, offering you a unique and peaceful getaway.

Night Stay Itinerary

  • Kolakham - 1 Night
  • Kafergaon - 1 Night
  • Kagey - 1 Night
  • Ramdhura - 1 Night

Pickup & Drop Locations

  • New Jalpaiguri Railway Station (NJP)
  • Bagdogra Airport
  • Siliguri Junction

Detailed Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Transfer to Kolakham

Pickup from NJP Railway Station, Tenzing Norgay Bus Stand, or Bagdogra Airport.

Enjoy a scenic drive to Kolakham via:

  • Gajoldoba, if time permits.
  • Gorubathan and Ambiok Tea Garden, where lush greenery surrounds you.
  • A beautiful stop at Paparkheti, followed by the picturesque Chell River.
  • Pass through enchanting pine forests.

Arrive at Kolakham for an overnight stay.

Day 2: Kolakham to Kafergaon

After a hearty breakfast, proceed to Kafergaon.

  • Visit Changey Falls, the biggest waterfall in Kalimpong.
  • Explore the Lava Monastery and enjoy the view from the I Love Lava View Point.
  • Stroll through the Lava Local Market to experience local culture.
  • End the day with a visit to Lolegaon Canopy Walk and witness the serene pine forests.

Overnight stay at Kafergaon.

Day 3: Kafergaon to Kagey

After breakfast, head to Kagey via the picturesque village of Rishop.

  • Enjoy the tranquility of Nokdara Lake and explore Pedong Market.
  • In the evening, relax around a bonfire or have a BBQ (at your own cost).

Overnight stay at Kagey.

Day 4: Kagey to Ramdhura

Post-breakfast, depart for Ramdhura, stopping along the way at:

  • Delo Park, offering panoramic views of Kalimpong.
  • Hanuman Tok, a famous temple and viewpoint.
  • Science Centre for a brief educational tour.
  • Ramdhura View Point for a stunning look at the mountains and valleys.

Overnight stay at Ramdhura.

Day 5: Departure

After breakfast, depart for NJP Railway Station, Siliguri Junction, or New Mal Junction.

Enroute, stop at Jalsha Bungalow and Jalsha Viewpoint for breathtaking views.

Continue the journey with a scenic drive along the Sevoke Tista View, where the river meanders through lush landscapes.

Tour ends with wonderful memories!
