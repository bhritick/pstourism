Offbeat Kalimpong Package Tour

4 Nights - 5 Days

PST Holidayz with Pather Sandhane Tourism

Book Now

Pick-up & Drop: NJP (New Jalpaiguri Railway Station) / Bagdogra (IXB) / Siliguri (SLG)

Night Stay:

  • Kolakham - 1 Night
  • Kafergaon - 1 Night
  • Kagey - 1 Night
  • Ramdhura - 1 Night

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Pickup and Kolakham Sightseeing

Pick up from NJP / Bagdogra / Siliguri and proceed to Kolakham via Gajoldoba (if time permits), Gorubathan, Ambiok Tea Garden, Paparkheti, and Chell River. Enjoy the beautiful pine forests en route. Overnight stay at Kolakham.

Day 2: Kolakham to Kafergaon

After breakfast, head to Kafergaon with sightseeing at Changey Falls (Kalimpong's biggest waterfall), Lava Monastery, Lava Local Market, and Lolegaon Canopy Walk. Enjoy the scenic pine forest sights. Overnight stay at Kafergaon.

Day 3: Kafergaon to Kagey

Proceed to Kagey after breakfast, visiting Rishop and Nokdara Lake on the way. In the evening, enjoy a bonfire or BBQ at your own expense. Night stay at Kagey.

Day 4: Kagey to Ramdhura

After breakfast, proceed to Ramdhura via Delo Park, Hanuman Tok, Science Centre, and Ramdhura View Point. Overnight stay at Ramdhura.

Day 5: Departure

After breakfast, return to NJP / Siliguri / New Mal Junction, visiting Jalsa Banglow, Jalsa View Point, and Sevoke Tista View en route. The tour ends with sweet memories!
