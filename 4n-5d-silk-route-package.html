Tour Itinerary

Experience exceptional service with Pather Sandhane Tourism on this enchanting 4-night, 5-day Silk Route package.

Day 1: Arrival in Sillerygaon

  • Pickup from NJP/IXB (New Jalpaiguri Railway Station/Bagdogra Airport)
  • Transfer to Sillerygaon
  • Enjoy a free day to explore the serene and offbeat location of Sillerygaon
  • Overnight stay at Sillerygaon

Day 2: Sillerygaon to Rorethang/Rishikhola/Lingtam (Optional)

  • Transfer to one of the optional destinations: Rorethang, Rishikhola, or Lingtam
  • Sightseeing en route, depending on the destination
  • Overnight stay at the chosen location

Day 3: Transfer to Zuluk

  • Travel from Rishikhola/Rorethang/Lingtam to Zuluk
  • Enjoy sightseeing along the way, including:
    • Kue Khola Waterfalls
    • Aritar Lake
    • Zigzag Road
  • Overnight stay at Zuluk

Day 4: Zuluk to Gangtok

  • Transfer to Gangtok with sightseeing en route, including:
    • Nathang Valley
    • Elephant Lake
    • Changu Lake
    • Baba Mandir
    • Nathula Pass
  • Overnight stay at Gangtok

Day 5: Departure

  • After breakfast, check out from the hotel
  • Transfer to NJP station for onward journey
