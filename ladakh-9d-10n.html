Tour Overview

Join us for a mesmerizing 7 Nights / 8 Days tour of Ladakh, organized by Pather Sandhane Tourism (PS Tourism), an ISO 9001:2015 certified company powered by IQ PLUS VLOGS.

Tour Itinerary

Day 01: Arrival - Jammu to Srinagar

This day is for acclimatization, leisure, and final preparations for the trip. Rest well. Overnight stay at Srinagar.

Day 02: Srinagar to Kargil Via Sonmarg (202 Kms, 7 Hrs)

After breakfast, check out from the Srinagar hotel and drive to Kargil via Sonamarg, one of the most scenic drives from Srinagar to Leh. Enroute, visit Namika-la, Mulbekh Chamba statue, Lamayuru Monastery, Kargil War Museum, and Zoji'la Pass. Overnight at Kargil.

Day 03: Kargil to Leh (217 Kms, 7.5 Hrs)

After breakfast, drive to Leh via Lamayuru Monastery and Alchi Monastery. Explore the ancient art and frescoes of 1000 years old. Overnight stay at Shey/Leh.

Day 04: Leh Local Sightseeing

Today, explore the vibrant capital of Ladakh. Visit Gurudwara Pathar Sahib, Magnetic Hill, the confluence of Indus and Zanskar Rivers, Hall of Fame, Spituk Monastery, Leh Palace, and Shanti Stupa. Enjoy the evening at the local market. Overnight at Shey/Leh.

Day 05: Shey/Leh to Hundar via Khardungla Pass (125 kms, 6 hrs)

Drive through the famous Khardungla Pass to Nubra Valley. Visit Diskit Monastery and explore the silver sand dunes of Hunder. Enjoy camel rides and adventure activities. Overnight stay at Hunder/Deskit.

Day 06: Hundar/Deskit to Pangong Tso (165 kms, 6 hrs)

Early morning departure for Pangong Lake. Enroute, witness breathtaking scenery and the famous 3 Idiots point at the lake. Overnight stay at a tent/camp near Pangong Tso.

Day 07: Pangong Tso to Shey/Leh via Chang-La Pass (220 kms, 7 hrs)

Return to Shey/Leh via Chang-La Pass. Visit Changla Baba Temple and spend the evening at the Leh market. Overnight stay at Shey/Leh.

Day 08: Leh to Kargil (Night Stay at Kargil)

Drive back to Kargil for an overnight stay.

Day 09: Kargil to Srinagar (Local)

Return to Srinagar and enjoy local sightseeing. Overnight stay in Srinagar.

Day 10: Drop at Jammu

After breakfast, drop at Jammu Airport. Farewell to fellow travelers and the magical land of Ladakh.
