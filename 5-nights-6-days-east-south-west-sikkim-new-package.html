Day-Wise Itinerary for Gangtok Tour

Day 1: Arrival in Gangtok

Pickup Point: NJP Railway Station/Bagdogra Airport (as per client preference).
Check-in: Deluxe Hotel in Gangtok.
Relax and explore nearby markets in the evening (MG Marg is highly recommended).
Meals: Lunch & Dinner.

Day 2: Gangtok Sightseeing

Visit:
Tsomgo Lake: A serene glacial lake at an altitude of 12,310 ft.
Baba Mandir: A popular pilgrimage site with breathtaking views.
Nathula Pass: (optional, at additional cost) Witness the Indo-China border and snow-clad mountains.
Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner.

Day-Wise Itinerary for Gangtok-Ravangla-Pelling Tour

Day 3: Gangtok to Ravangla

Morning Transfer: Transfer to Ravangla (approx. 2.5 hours).
En Route: Enjoy the lush greenery of the Temi Tea Garden.
Post Check-In: Visit the serene Buddha Park and marvel at its stunning architecture.
Optional: Namchi Chardham (at additional cost).
Overnight Stay: Ravangla hotel.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner.

Day 4: Ravangla to Pelling

Morning Transfer: After breakfast, transfer to Pelling (approx. 2 hours).
Check-In: Check-in to your hotel and enjoy a relaxing evening.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner.

Day-Wise Itinerary for Pelling Sightseeing and Departure

Day 5: Pelling Sightseeing

Explore:
Glass Sky Walk: Enjoy a thrilling walk with stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
Rimbi River & Rimbi Orange Garden: Ideal spots for a tranquil nature retreat.
Kecheopalri Lake: A sacred lake known for its serene environment.
Darap Village: Immerse yourself in local Sikkimese culture.
Kanchenjunga Waterfalls: A spectacular sight nestled amidst lush greenery.
Monastery: Experience peace and spiritual vibes.
Overnight Stay: At the hotel.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner.

Day 6: Departure

Check-Out: From the hotel.
Transfer: To NJP Railway Station/Bagdogra Airport (as per client preference).
End of the Trip: With cherished memories of Sikkim.
Meals: Breakfast.
