Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Transfer to Mirik

Pick-up from NJP Station/Bagdogra Airport and transfer to Mirik, Rangbhang. Spend the evening by the Rangbhang River.

Day 2: Mirik to Darjeeling

Explore Mirik with visits to Gopaldhara Tea Estate, Mirik Lake, and Pashupati Market. Transfer to Darjeeling for the evening.

Day 3: Darjeeling Sightseeing

Visit Tiger Hill at sunrise, followed by Batasia Loop, Ghoom Monastery, Peace Pagoda, and more Darjeeling highlights.

Day 4: Free Day in Darjeeling

Spend a leisurely day in Darjeeling or enjoy a joy ride on the Toy Train.

Day 5: Transfer to Gangtok

Transfer from Darjeeling to Gangtok. Explore MG Marg and Lal Market in the evening.

Day 6: Gangtok to Lachen (North Sikkim)

Begin your journey to North Sikkim with sightseeing en route to Lachen.

Day 7: Gurudongmar Lake & Lachung

Visit the stunning Gurudongmar Lake, then transfer to Lachung for an overnight stay.

Day 8: Yumthang Valley & Zero Point

Explore Yumthang Valley and Zero Point, followed by a return to Gangtok.

Day 9: Rest or East Sikkim Excursion

Relax in Gangtok or opt for a visit to Tsomgo Lake, Baba Mandir, and Nathula Pass.

Day 10: Departure

After breakfast, transfer to NJP Station/Bagdogra Airport for your return journey.
