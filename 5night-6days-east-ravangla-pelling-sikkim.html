5 Nights 6 Days Sikkim Tour Package

Offered by Pather Sandhane Tourism (PST Holidayz) - An ISO 9001:2015 Certified Travel Organization

Website: www.pstourism.in

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Transfer to Gangtok

Arrival: Arrive at NJP Station / IXB Airport.

Transfer: Proceed to Gangtok, the vibrant capital of Sikkim.

Overnight Stay: Relax and unwind with an overnight stay in Gangtok.

Day 2: Exploring East Sikkim

Tsomgo Lake: Visit this stunning glacial lake, surrounded by snowy peaks.

Baba Mandir: A revered shrine dedicated to Baba Harbhajan Singh.

Nathula Pass: Journey to this high-altitude pass, which serves as a gateway to Tibet.

Overnight Stay: Return to Gangtok for another night.

Day 3: Transfer to Ravangla

Journey: Travel from Gangtok to Ravangla.

Sightseeing:

  • Namchi Chardham: Explore this sacred pilgrimage site with replicas of Char Dham.
  • Temi Tea Garden: Stroll through Sikkim's only tea garden, renowned for its quality tea.
  • Sandruptse: Visit the giant statue of Guru Padmasambhava and enjoy panoramic views.

Overnight Stay: Stay the night in Ravangla.

Day 4: Transfer to Pelling

Journey: Travel from Ravangla to Pelling.

Sightseeing:

  • Glass Skywalk: Experience the thrilling glass bridge offering breathtaking views.
  • Old Monastery: Visit one of the region's oldest monasteries, rich in history and culture.

Overnight Stay: Enjoy a comfortable night in Pelling.

Day 5: Pelling Sightseeing

Rimbi River: Admire the scenic beauty of this river.

Rimbi Orange Garden: Visit this picturesque orchard.

Khecheopalri Lake: Explore this sacred lake surrounded by lush forests.

Kanchenjunga Waterfalls: Marvel at the majestic waterfalls cascading from the mountains.

Darap Village: Discover the charm of this traditional village.

Overnight Stay: Spend another night in Pelling.

Day 6: Departure

Breakfast: Enjoy your last breakfast in Sikkim.

Drop-off: Transfer to NJP Station / IXB Airport / SLG for your onward journey.

Inclusions

  • Meals: All meals from lunch on Day 1 to breakfast on the day of checkout.
  • Accommodation: Quality lodging throughout the trip.
  • Transportation: Experienced hill driver and driver allowances.
  • Permits: Necessary permits for travel.

  • Other Expenses: Anything not specifically mentioned in the package.
