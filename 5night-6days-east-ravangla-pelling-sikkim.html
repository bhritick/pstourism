Tour Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival at NJP Station / IXB Airport. Transfer to Gangtok. Overnight stay at Gangtok.
  • Day 2: Explore East Sikkim. Visit Tsomgo Lake, Baba Mandir, and Nathula Pass. Overnight stay at Gangtok.
  • Day 3: Transfer from Gangtok to Ravangla. Sightseeing includes Namchi Chardham, Temi Tea Garden, and Sandruptse. Overnight stay in Ravangla.
  • Day 4: Transfer to Pelling. Visit the Glass Skywalk and one of the oldest monasteries in the region. Overnight stay at Pelling.
  • Day 5: Pelling sightseeing: Rimbi River, Rimbi Orange Garden, Khecheopalri Lake, Kanchenjunga Waterfalls, and Darap Village. Overnight stay in Pelling.
  • Day 6: After breakfast, drop-off at NJP Station/IXB/SLG.
