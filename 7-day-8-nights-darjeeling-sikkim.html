Tour Details

Duration: 7 Nights 8 Days | 8 Nights 9 Days | 9 Nights 10 Days

Itinerary includes:

  • 2 Nights in Darjeeling
  • 3 Nights in Gangtok
  • 2 Nights in North Sikkim
  • One Day East Sikkim sightseeing: Tsomgo Lake, Baba Mandir, Nathula Pass

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Mirik

Pickup from NJP station/Airport and transfer to offbeat Mirik, Rangbhang. Check-in at Hotel/Homestay. Enjoy the evening by the Rangbhang river.

Day 2: Mirik to Darjeeling

Mirik sightseeing: Gopaldhara Tea Estate, Mirik Lake, Pashupati Market, Bokar Monastery, Simana View Point. Evening in Darjeeling Mall Market.

Day 3: Darjeeling Sightseeing

Early morning visit to Tiger Hill for sunrise. After breakfast: Batasia Loop, Ghoom Monastery, Peace Pagoda, Japanese Temple, and more.

Day 4: Free Day in Darjeeling

Enjoy a leisure day in Darjeeling, with options for a joy ride.

Day 5: Transfer to Gangtok

Pickup from Darjeeling, explore High Court Museum, and MG Marg in the evening.

Day 6: North Sikkim

Start journey to North Sikkim, transfer to Lachen with sightseeing on the way.

Day 7: Lachen to Lachung

Transfer to Lachung with sightseeing of Gurudongmar Lake.

Day 8: Yumthang Valley

Explore Yumthang Valley and Zero Points. Transfer back to Gangtok.

Day 9: East Sikkim

Explore East Sikkim: Changu Lake, Baba Mandir, Nathula Pass. Option to rest in Gangtok.

Day 10: Departure

After breakfast, transfer to NJP station/Airport.
