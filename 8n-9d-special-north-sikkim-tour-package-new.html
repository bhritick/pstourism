Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Pickup from NJP & Transfer to Gangtok

  • Pick-up: From NJP Railway Station.
  • Transfer: To Gangtok.
  • Overnight Stay: Gangtok.

Day 2: Journey to Dzongu

  • After Breakfast: Transfer to Dzongu.
  • Highlights: Lush landscapes, Mount Kanchenjunga views, and Lepcha cultural experiences.
  • Overnight Stay: Dzongu.

Day 3: Dzongu Sightseeing

  • Explore Thingvong Village and Lingthem Village for breathtaking views of Kanchenjunga.
  • Experience the unique Lepcha culture.
  • Overnight Stay: Dzongu.

Day 4: Transfer to Lachen

  • After Breakfast: Scenic drive to Lachen.
  • Overnight Stay: Lachen.

Day 5: Lachen Sightseeing & Transfer to Lachung

  • Visit Gurudongmar Lake, one of the world's highest lakes.
  • Transfer: To Lachung after the visit.
  • Overnight Stay: Lachung.

Day 6: Lachung Sightseeing

  • Visit Yumthang Valley, the Valley of Flowers.
  • Explore Zero Point for stunning snow-capped mountain views.
  • Overnight Stay: Lachung.

Day 7: Mount Katao & Songlaphu Lake

  • Visit Mount Katao for pristine beauty and scenic views.
  • Explore Songlaphu Lake and rejuvenate in natural hot springs.
  • Overnight Stay: Lachung.

Day 8: Return to Gangtok

  • After Breakfast: Transfer back to Gangtok.
  • Overnight Stay: Gangtok.

Day 9: Departure

  • After Breakfast: Drop-off at NJP Railway Station for your return journey.
