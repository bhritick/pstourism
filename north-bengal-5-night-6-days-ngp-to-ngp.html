4 Night 5 Day Kalimpong & Darjeeling Tour Plan

Experience the beauty of Kalimpong & Darjeeling with Pather Sandhane Tourism

Book Now

Day 1 - Arrival at Kalimpong

We will pick you up from NJP station and transfer you to your homestay in Kalimpong. Relax and unwind after your journey.

Day 2 - Kalimpong Sightseeing

  • Ramdhura
  • Durpin Monastery
  • Hanuman Temple
  • Deolo Hill
  • Durpin Dara
  • Golf Course

Afternoon free for personal activities. Overnight stay at Kalimpong.

Day 3 - Kalimpong to Darjeeling

After breakfast, we will start our journey to Darjeeling. On the way, enjoy sightseeing at:

  • Lamahata Eco Park
  • Lovers View Point
  • Peshok Tea Garden
  • Ghumna Dara View Point

Evening at leisure to enjoy Darjeeling Mall. Overnight stay at Darjeeling.

Day 4 - Darjeeling Sightseeing

Explore the beauty of Darjeeling with the full-day sightseeing tour. For more details, please refer to the Darjeeling Sightseeing PDF provided by us.

Day 5 - Departure

After breakfast, we will drop you back at NJP station for your onward journey with unforgettable memories of Kalimpong and Darjeeling.
